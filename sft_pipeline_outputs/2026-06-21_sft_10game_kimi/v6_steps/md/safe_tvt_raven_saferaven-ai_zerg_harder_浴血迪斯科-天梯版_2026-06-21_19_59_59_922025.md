# Summary

The build opens with a gas-first Reaper expand into a standard Rax-Factory-Starport tech path, getting an early natural orbital and pumping Hellions for map control before transitioning into Raven and Siege Tank production out of a tech-labbed Factory and Starport. The mid-game scales hard with five Rax (mixing tech labs and reactors), double Starport for Viking and Medivac production, and double EBay for +1/+1 bio upgrades alongside Stim and Combat Shields, creating a classic bio-tank deathball with Raven support and air control. The late-game aims for a maxed-out 3-base bio-mechanical army with continuous marine production, tank and liberator siege potential, and armory upgrades pushing the composition toward overwhelming sustained pressure and a decisive timing push before the opponent can stabilize.

# Details

[Step 1] Keep worker production rolling nonstop while throwing down the first depot and both refineries to get gas flowing early. Drop the first rax as mineral income ramps up, setting the foundation for a tech-heavy opening.

[Step 2] Morph the CC into an orbital and throw down the factory, then queue up a couple of reapers for early map pressure while keeping worker production rolling. Add a depot for supply, then transition into hellion production from the factory as you take your natural expansion. Keep the SCVs flowing and drop another depot to stay ahead of supply, rounding out with a third reaper and a second hellion to secure map control going into the mid-game.

[Step 3] Throw down a starport and keep worker production rolling while adding tech labs to your factory and first rax, then start a cyclone out of the factory. Add a tech lab to the starport as well and morph your CC into an orbital to unlock macro abilities and keep the economy flowing.

[Step 4] Throw down the second, third, and fourth Refineries to get your gas economy rolling, then queue up a Raven from the starport and a Siege Tank from the factory to start building your mid-game army. Keep worker production flowing with several SCVs, and secure the third CC to set up your expansion timing.

[Step 5] Keep worker production rolling with several SCVs while throwing down a couple depots to stay ahead of supply. Queue up a Raven from the starport and a Siege Tank from the factory to build out your core army, then add a second starport to start ramping up air production.

[Step 6] Add a second and third rax while keeping worker production rolling and throwing down a couple depots for supply. Queue up another tank from the factory and start building out the viking count with 3 vikings from the starport to establish air control and set up for a potential push.

[Step 7] Morph the natural CC into an orbital while keeping worker production rolling, then add a reactor to the first rax and a reactor to the starport to pump out medivacs and marines faster. Start siege tank production at the factory and queue up a viking from the port to begin establishing air control and a solid mid-game army core.

[Step 8] Throw down two e-bays to get +1 upgrades rolling, then add a third and fourth geyser to support the tech transition. Keep workers pumping while swapping the rax over to a reactor, and drop a depot to stay ahead on supply.

[Step 9] Kick off stim research at the rax tech lab while keeping worker production rolling with several SCVs. Queue up a couple of vikings from the starport to start gaining air control, throw down two depots to keep supply ahead of your production, and add a second rax to begin ramping up your bio output.

[Step 10] Throw down a second and third rax alongside a couple depots to shore up supply while keeping worker production rolling. Start both +1 attack and +1 armor at the ebay to get your bio upgrades rolling, setting up for a strong mid-game timing.

[Step 11] Keep worker production rolling with a few SCVs while the factory pumps out another tank and the rax churns out a wave of marines. Queue up a medivac from the starport to start building your air support backbone.

[Step 12] Throw down a couple depots to keep supply from capping while queuing up two medivacs from the starport. Keep worker production rolling at the CC and reinforce with a wave of four marines to build up the bio core.

[Step 13] Throw down a couple of depots and slap reactors on two rax to start cranking out marines, while adding a tank from the factory and a medivac from the starport to round out the core bio-mechanical army. Drop one more depot to keep supply ahead of the production curve.

[Step 14] Keep the marine ball growing with a wave of fresh recruits while throwing down depots to stay ahead of supply. Start the armory to unlock future upgrades and pump out a pair of libs from the starport for map control and siege potential.

[Step 15] Research combat shields while throwing down a few depots to keep supply ahead of your production. Train a couple more Marines, queue up a viking from the starport, and add a tank to the factory line to keep your mech count growing.

[Step 16] Keep the rax pumping marines non-stop while throwing down a couple depots to stay ahead of supply. Start a viking out of the starport to begin establishing air control and scout for any incoming threats.

[Step 17] Flood out a wave of Marines from multiple rax while queuing up 2 Vikings at the starport to contest air control, add another Siege Tank from the factory, and start mech +1 / vehicle weapons at the armory to keep your tank and viking damage scaling into the mid-game.

[Step 18] Throw down a factory to start transitioning into mech or support production, then queue up a wave of marines from the rax to keep pressure on the map. Toss down a depot to stay ahead of supply, and kick off infantry +1 attack so your bio scales into the mid-game.

[Step 19] Keep marine production rolling out of the rax while queuing up a couple vikings from the starport to contest air control and scout for drops or BC transitions.

[Step 20] Pump out a wave of Marines from the rax while adding a Siege Tank from the factory for some backbone, then queue up a few more marines and start researching infantry armor to toughen up the bio ball moving forward.

[Step 21] Keep the rax humming with a steady stream of marines while queuing up a couple of vikings from the starport to contest air control. Meanwhile, slap a tech lab onto the factory to unlock siege tank or cyclone production for the mid-game push.

[Step 22] This is a methodical bio-tank macro build that plays the long game with a heavy emphasis on economic scaling, layered tech development, and overwhelming mid-game pressure through a classic Raven-supported deathball. It leans defensive in the opening before transitioning into a relentless, upgrade-fueled siege tempo that aims to break the opponent before they can match the production or tech. Use this gameplan as your strategic baseline -- adapt your decisions based on what you scout and how the game unfolds.
