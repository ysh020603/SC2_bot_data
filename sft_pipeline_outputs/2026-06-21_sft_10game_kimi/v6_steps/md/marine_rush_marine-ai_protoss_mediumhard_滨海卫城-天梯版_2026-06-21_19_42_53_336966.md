# Summary

The bot opens with a standard 3-rax marine setup, getting an orbital up on the first CC while powering workers and flooding marines, though notably skipping the natural expansion entirely in favor of pure on-base production. The mid-game is pure all-in bio aggression with no tech labs, no engineering bays, and no third base—just reactors and tech labs swapped around on a growing mass of rax as the marine count snowballs toward critical mass. The late-game plan is nonexistent by design: this is a maxed-out marine timing push or bust, banking on overwhelming the opponent with raw bio numbers before any splash, upgrades, or tech transitions ever come online.
