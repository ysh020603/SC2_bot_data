# Summary

Old Rusty opens with a standard rax-first into quick natural CC, getting both orbitals up early while transitioning through a 1-1-1 with Factory tech lab into Siege Tanks before adding the Starport and mass rax with reactors. The mid-game spikes hard into bio production with Stim and Combat Shields, running a bio-mech hybrid that leans heavier into marine flood with medivac support and tank anchoring, taking a third CC around the time the reactor swap finishes across the barracks. The late-game plan is pure bio deathball with tank splash backup, maxing out on a 3-base economy and overwhelming the opponent through sheer marine count before they can tech into anything that hard-counters the swarm.
