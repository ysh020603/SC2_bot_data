# Summary

The build kicks off with a standard rax-first opening into a quick natural CC, grabbing early gas and using a reaper for scout pressure while setting up a bunker for safety, then transitions into a Factory with a reactor and a tech-labbed rax to start stim research on the way to a 2-1-1 setup. Production scales hard with three additional rax thrown down to form a 5-rax core, reactors slapped across the board to flood marines, and the starport pumping medivacs while the factory churns widow mines for map control and defensive traps, with +1 and combat shield coming online to power up the bio ball for a marine-medivac-mine midgame timing. The endgame plan leans into overwhelming sustained production from a maxed-out 5-rax reactor marine flood backed by medivac support and widow mine splash, aiming to drown the opponent in a sea of bio rather than tech-switching, with the economy humming on two-base saturation plus refineries to keep the war machine rolling.
