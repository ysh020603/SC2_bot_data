# Summary

The build opens with a standard rax-first into quick natural expansion, dropping a Factory with a Tech Lab for early Siege Tank production while keeping worker production rolling across two bases. The mid-game powers up hard with multiple rax—some on Reactors for marine flood, others on Tech Labs for Combat Shields—while a second Factory comes online to crank out tanks, establishing a classic bio-tank core that sieges up and controls space until the third CC lands. The late-game plan is to max out on a marine-tank deathball, banking gas with extra Refineries while continuously reinforcing, aiming to overwhelm with sustained production and siege pressure rather than teching to air or ghosts.
