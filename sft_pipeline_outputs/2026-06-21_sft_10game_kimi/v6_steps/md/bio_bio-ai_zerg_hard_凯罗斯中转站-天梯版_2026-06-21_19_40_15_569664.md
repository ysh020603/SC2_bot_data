# Summary

The build opens with a standard 1-1-1 setup: two rax with a reaper for early scout pressure, quick natural CC behind a bunker, and a factory teching into tank while the starport comes online for medivac support. The mid-game powers up through stim and combat shield upgrades, scaling to five rax total with a mix of tech labs and reactors to flood marine-marauder bio, supported by a medivac-heavy starport and viking for air control, playing a sustained pressure style with a third CC rolling by the time the bio ball moves out. The endgame aims for a maxed-out 3-base bio deathball with full medivac reinforcement, leveraging constant production and drop potential to overwhelm through attrition rather than a hard tech switch.

# Details

[Step 1] Keep workers rolling out of the CC and throw down a depot to avoid supply block, then grab a refinery to start gas production. Drop the first rax and quickly add a second, with a reaper coming out to scout and apply early pressure.

[Step 2] Morph the main CC into an orbital, then take the natural expansion to set up a second base. Keep worker production rolling and throw down a depot to stay ahead of supply. Train a couple of marines for early defense while adding a bunker at the natural to hold off any aggression. Finish by putting a reactor on the rax and throwing down the first factory to start transitioning into factory tech.

[Step 3] Keep worker production rolling from the CC while training a steady stream of marines out of the rax. Throw down the factory tech lab and morph an OC to unlock scan and mule energy, setting up for the upcoming tech transition.

[Step 4] Keep the marine production rolling while the factory pumps out its first tank. Throw down a couple of depots to stay ahead of supply, then grab a second gas to fuel the tech transition. Add a tech lab onto the rax so stim or combat shield can come online soon, and queue up a few more SCVs to keep the economy ticking.

[Step 5] Throw down a starport to open up air options while queuing up a couple of marines and several SCVs to keep the economy and army ticking. Research concussive shells at the tech lab to give marauders their slowing grenades, then add a second rax for more bio production and top off with a depot to avoid supply block.

[Step 6] Keep worker production rolling while throwing down a couple of depots to avoid supply block. Add another tank from the factory and round out the rax output with a few more marines to reinforce the frontline or prepare for a push.

[Step 7] Start stim research and add a tech lab to the rax while keeping worker production rolling. Train a wave of marines and queue up the first medivac to prepare for a bio push, then throw down a depot to keep supply ahead of the production curve.

[Step 8] Take the third CC while keeping worker production rolling, then grab combat shields to toughen up the bio. Throw down a couple more rax for extra production and start cranking out marauders and a wave of marines, while adding a medivac to keep the push healthy—don't forget to drop a depot so you don't get supply blocked.

[Step 9] Add a third rax and keep the marine-marauder production rolling to build out your bio force, throw down a couple depots to stay ahead of supply, then slap a reactor onto one of the rax for faster marine output. Start building out your starport with a viking to gain air control and scout for incoming threats.

[Step 10] Keep marine production rolling and add a few marauders to the mix for extra punch, throw down a couple depots to stay ahead of supply, and morph an OC on the natural to get that economy humming. Finish by adding a reactor to the rax so you can flood even more bio onto the field.

[Step 11] Keep the marine pump flowing while mixing in a couple of marauders for added beef against armored targets. Add a reactor to the starport to start doubling air production, and throw down both your third and fourth refineries to support the growing gas demands of your tech and unit mix.

[Step 12] Throw down a second rax to ramp up bio production, then queue up a marauder and a wave of marines alongside a second marauder to build out your core army. Start a medivac at the starport to get drop potential rolling and support the bio ball as it moves out.

[Step 13] Throw down a depot to keep supply ahead of the incoming bio ball, then rally a medivac from the starport to support the push. Queue up a wave of marines and a couple of marauders out of the rax to reinforce the frontline, and drop two more depots so you don't get supply blocked as the army swells.

[Step 14] Keep the rax humming with a mix of marines and marauders while queuing up 2 medivacs from the starport to support the bio ball; throw down a couple depots to stay ahead of supply as the army rounds out for the mid-game push.

[Step 15] Keep the rax hot by pumping out a mixed bio force—several Marines and a couple of Marauders—to hold the front, while throwing down a depot to avoid supply block. Meanwhile, get a Viking and a medivac rolling out of the starport to establish air control and set up drop play.

[Step 16] This is a classic macro bio snowball that plays the long game through relentless marine-marauder production, layered upgrades, and medivac-powered attrition to grind opponents down across the map. A solid, no-frills 1-1-1 into 5-rax bio deathball that leans on constant reinforcement and drop pressure rather than gimmicks or hard tech switches. Use this gameplan as your strategic baseline -- adapt your decisions based on what you scout and how the game unfolds.
