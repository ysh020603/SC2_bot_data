# Summary

The opening rocks a rax-first into fast expand with a Reaper scout, dropping an early Factory with Tech Lab to start churning out Tanks while walling off with depots and adding a bunker for safety. The mid-game kicks into high gear with a third base and a massive factory count spiking up to five-plus, stacking Tech Labs everywhere and pumping Tanks nonstop while the Armory gets +1 vehicle weapons rolling and a Starport with Reactor comes online for eventual air support. The late-game deathball hard transitions into Thor-Tank-Viking mech, seizing air control with mass Vikings while the economy stretches to four bases, aiming to max out on a beefy siege-based army and slowly choke the opponent out with overwhelming firepower and sustained production.

# Details

[Step 1] Keep worker production rolling from the CC while throwing down the first rax and getting gas online with a Refinery. Add a couple depots to stay ahead of supply and smooth out the early macro as you transition into producing units.

[Step 2] Morph an orbital on the main CC, then train a reaper for early scout pressure while keeping worker production rolling. Drop the second CC for an early expo, throw down the first refinery, and start a factory with a tech lab shortly after. Add a depot to keep supply clean and continue pumping several SCVs to saturate both bases.

[Step 3] Morph your main CC into an orbital to unlock mule energy, then throw down the first refinery to start gas income. Queue up a couple of tanks from the factory while keeping worker production rolling with several SCVs, and add a bunker for early defense. Drop the second factory to ramp up tank production heading into the mid-game.

[Step 4] Throw down a Refinery to get gas flowing for factory tech, then churn out several SCVs to keep the economy humming while the first Siege Tank rolls out. Add a couple of depots to stay ahead of supply as production ramps up.

[Step 5] Throw down a wall of depots to clear supply block and make room for a massive army, keep worker production rolling with a couple more SCVs, and add a tech lab to the factory to unlock tank or hellbat tech.

[Step 6] Throw down an armory to unlock vehicle upgrades and get your mech ball rolling, queue up a couple of tanks from the factory, and keep the worker production flowing with several SCVs across your bases. Drop your third CC to secure the economic foundation for a heavier mid-game push.

[Step 7] Throw down 4 factories to establish a heavy mech production backbone while keeping SCV production rolling. Start cranking out the first Siege Tank as the factories come online, setting up for a powerful tank-based mid-game push.

[Step 8] Keep worker production rolling while adding a starport and throwing down a depot to stay ahead on supply. The factories get their tech labs online, and a couple of tanks come out to start building that siege-based core. This is the mech transition taking shape—tanks for the frontline, with the port setting up for eventual air support or medivac follow-up.

[Step 9] Morph the CC into an orbital to unlock mule production and scouting, then throw down two refineries to secure gas for the upcoming mech transition. Add three factories and slap a reactor on the starport to start cranking out production, while kicking off mech +1 at the armory. Keep worker production rolling with a few SCVs to maintain economic momentum.

[Step 10] Throw down 2 factories and start pumping tanks immediately, with 4 Siege Tanks rolling out to anchor your army. Keep supply from blocking by dropping depots in between, then add tech labs onto both factories so you can keep the tank production flowing with proper upgrades.

[Step 11] Throw down a starport to open up air tech options while keeping tank production rolling with several Siege Tanks coming out of the factory. Drop a wave of depots to clear supply block and keep the production flowing, then add tech labs onto the factories so you can keep cranking out tanks with proper support.

[Step 12] Throw down 5 factories all at once to kick the mech production into overdrive, then queue up a couple of tanks and start teching two of the factories with tech labs. Take a fourth CC in the back to keep the economy rolling and support the heavy gas demands of this factory-heavy transition.

[Step 13] Push out a tank and a wave of Thors from the factory while throwing down depots to clear the supply block and make room for the heavy mech army.

[Step 14] Spam down a wall of depots to clear the supply block and make room for the heavy mech army, queue up 4 Thors from the factory, and morph an OC while grabbing a couple more gas to keep the thor production rolling.

[Step 15] Throw down the armory and start mech +1 vehicle weapons to power up the heavy hitters. The factory churns out a wave of Thors while the starport adds a handful of vikings for air control, setting up a beefy mech deathball that can push across the map.

[Step 16] Mass produce vikings out of the starport to seize air dominance and counter any enemy colossi or air threats, queue up a tank for ground support, and drop a new CC to expand the economy and support this heavy production.

[Step 17] This is a hardcore macro mech build that drowns the opponent in a tidal wave of factories, slowly suffocating them under an avalanche of Thor-Tank-Viking deathball firepower. It embodies the classic "more factories than sense" Terran mech fantasy, trading early proactivity for an unstoppable late-game siege juggernaut that grinds forward through sheer production volume and upgrade-backed beef. Use this gameplan as your strategic baseline -- adapt your decisions based on what you scout and how the game unfolds.
