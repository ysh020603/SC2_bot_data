# Summary

The bot opens with a depot into rax foundation and quickly piles on more barracks, skipping gas and tech entirely to commit to a pure marine flood while taking an OC for macro. There is no mid-game tech transition or upgrade path—the build stays on a single mineral-focused production cycle with reactors and tech labs nowhere in sight, just constant marine churn from a growing rax count to hit a critical mass timing. The endgame plan is to max out on pure bio and slam into the opponent before any splash or tech answers come online, relying on overwhelming marine count and reinforcements to close the game with no expansion past the natural and no late-game transition in sight.
