# Summary

The opening is a standard 1-rax expand into a Factory with a Tech Lab for early Siege Tanks, then a Starport with Tech Lab into a quick Fusion Core for Battlecruiser production, with a natural taken early and constant SCV flow. In the mid game, the player scales up with extra Barracks, reactors, and a second Starport to pump Marines and Tanks while rushing out the first few BCs, researching Combat Shield and dropping a third base to support the gas-heavy tech. Late game, the composition relies on mass Battlecruisers backed by a Marine-Tank ground force, with three or more bases and multiple OCs for MULEs to sustain constant production and maxed-out timing pushes.

# Details

[Step 1] Start by dropping the first Supply Depot, then keep the Command Center constantly producing workers. As minerals allow, throw down the Barracks and immediately place a Refinery to start gathering gas. Continue pumping out SCVs without pause and add a second Depot so you don't get supply blocked as your economy ramps up.

[Step 2] Morph the first Command Center into an Orbital while taking the natural expansion, keep up SCV production and train a few Marines for defense, then drop a Refinery and start a Factory to move into mechanical tech.

[Step 3] Keep SCV production constant while throwing down a depot to avoid supply blocks, and train a handful of marines. Then drop a starport and a bunker for defense, and morph your main CC into an orbital to boost your economy.

[Step 4] Start a Barracks and a Refinery to ramp up tech, then slap a Tech Lab on the Factory while churning out several SCVs and a Marine for early defense. Drop a Fusion Core to set up Battlecruiser production, and finish by adding a Tech Lab to the Starport for access to high-tier air upgrades.

[Step 5] Drop a second Refinery for extra gas, keep pumping out SCVs, train a handful of Marines, and begin Siege Tank production from the Factory to build up your mech force.

[Step 6] Keep SCV production steady and keep Marines coming from the Barracks, throw down another depot to avoid supply blocks, then start building the first Battlecruiser from the Starport to apply immediate pressure.

[Step 7] Add multiple depots to free up supply, continue worker production, build a Siege Tank for defensive siege potential, and reinforce the bio force with a Marine to keep the army ticking.

[Step 8] Keep SCV and Marine production going while adding several depots to manage the supply, then start your first Siege Tank out of the Factory to gain early map control or defensive force.

[Step 9] Throw down an extra Barracks with a tech lab for future upgrades, while also adding a reactor to another Barracks to ramp up Marine production. Kick off a Battlecruiser from your existing Starport and follow up by building a second Starport with a tech lab for sustained BC output. Keep SCV production steady and drop supply depots to avoid blocks. From the Factory, roll out a Siege Tank for defense, and train a few Marines—starting with one and then reinforcing with several more—to hold the front.

[Step 10] Research Combat Shield for your bio forces while dropping an extra Barracks to boost Marine production. Train a handful of Marines, start a Battlecruiser from the Starport and a Siege Tank from the Factory, then throw down two depots to keep supply flowing.

[Step 11] Add a couple of depots to keep supply flowing, pump out a few marines for defense, and start building your first battlecruiser. Then drop two more depots and train an additional marine to maintain pressure.

[Step 12] Focus on building up your bio-mech army with a steady wave of marines, add a Siege Tank for defensive firepower, and then start producing your first Battlecruiser to apply pressure or secure map control.

[Step 13] Reinforce the army by pumping out several Marines while adding a batch of depots to keep up with the rising supply demand.

[Step 14] Keep pumping marines from the Barracks while starting your first Battlecruiser from the Starport and a Siege Tank from the Factory; don't forget to throw down an extra depot to support the growing army.

[Step 15] Add a depot to keep supply flowing, then pump out a wave of Marines for defense and map presence, while the Starport starts producing the first Battlecruiser for mid-game power.

[Step 16] Reinforce with a wave of Marines while also producing a single Siege Tank and starting up the first Battlecruiser to provide a powerful late-game threat.

[Step 17] Crank out a wave of Marines from your rax while the factory produces a Siege Tank, then immediately start your first Battlecruiser at the starport and keep reinforcing with more Marines as the BC finishes.

[Step 18] Keep the rax pumping out a wave of Marines while the Factory adds a Siege Tank to solidify your early army composition.

[Step 19] With two Starports already established, start producing Battlecruisers while using your Barracks to reinforce with a steady wave of Marines for ground support. Add another Depot to keep supply flowing as you queue up a second BC to follow the first.

[Step 20] Keep pumping marines out of the barracks to build a solid bio force, while the factory rolls out a siege tank for additional firepower. Throw down a supply depot to avoid supply block, and take another expansion to support the upcoming heavy tech. Then begin construction of a pair of battlecruisers from the starport, transitioning into a powerful late-game air army.

[Step 21] Expand to your natural and third bases, immediately converting both Command Centers to Orbital Commands for extra mules and supply. Meanwhile, keep up marine production with a few units, add an extra Barracks for more bio, and throw down a Refinery to secure gas for the Battlecruiser you start building in your Starport.

[Step 22] Morph the Command Center into an Orbital Command to enable MULEs and increased scanning, then pump out 8 Marines from the Barracks while beginning production on a Siege Tank from the Factory to solidify your position and set up for an early push.

[Step 23] Pump out a wave of Marines from the rax to build up a solid army, while adding two Refineries to secure the gas needed for tech upgrades.

[Step 24] Keep producing Marines and Siege Tanks from your Barracks and Factories while continuously building Battlecruisers from your Starports, and maintain SCV production and OC energy for MULEs. If you scout heavy anti-air like Corruptors or Phoenix, mix in Vikings or Thors for support; against mass ground like Ling-Bane, spread your Tanks to protect your Marines. As you secure a fourth base, consider adding Liberators or transitioning to mass BCs with Yamato and tactical jump for map dominance. Use this gameplan as your strategic baseline — adapt your decisions based on what you scout and how the game unfolds.
