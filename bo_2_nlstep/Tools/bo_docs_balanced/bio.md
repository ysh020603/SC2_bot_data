# Summary

The player opens with a Reaper off double Rax, takes an early natural expansion, adds a Factory for a Siege Tank, then drops two Starports while teching towards Stim and Combat Shield. In the mid game, they take a third base, scale up to five Rax mixing Reactors and Tech Labs, and pump Marines, Marauders, and Medivacs with a Viking for air control, hitting a timing push. The late game aims for a maxed bio ball on three bases, reinforcing nonstop with Medivac-supported infantry to overwhelm the opponent through sustained production.

# Details

[Step 1] Open with a Supply Depot to keep supply flowing, then queue up several SCVs from your Command Center while dropping your first two Barracks and the first Refinery. Once the Barracks finish, start producing a Reaper for early harassment and map control.

[Step 2] After morphing the first Command Center into an Orbital Command for better macro, keep SCV production going steadily and train a couple of Marines from the existing Barracks. Now is the perfect time to expand—drop another Command Center for your natural—and throw down a Supply Depot to avoid supply blocks. Add a Reactor on the Barracks to double Marine production, then start a Factory to unlock mech options, all while continuing worker production to prepare for the new base.

[Step 3] Keep SCV production steady while reinforcing with a few Marines, then add a Tech Lab to the Factory and place several Supply Depots to prevent supply blocks.

[Step 4] Morph the Command Center into an Orbital Command to lay down MULEs, then start training a handful of Marines from the Barracks while also producing a Siege Tank from the Factory. Add a second Refinery to boost gas income, attach a Tech Lab to the Barracks for future upgrades, and keep SCV production steady with a couple more workers.

[Step 5] Drop two Starports to transition into air production while adding another Barracks for extra infantry. Keep SCV production steady, research Concussive Shells to boost your Marauders, and train a couple of Marines to reinforce your army.

[Step 6] Keep SCV production constant while pumping out a siege tank from the factory. Train a few marines and one marauder for a solid bio-mech mix, and throw down depots to stay ahead on supply. Finally, start adding a tech lab on your rax to prepare for combat shield or stim.

[Step 7] Kick off Stimpack research from the Barracks tech lab while continuing to pump out a handful of Marines. Throw down a couple of depots to keep supply flowing, and maintain worker production by training several SCVs from the Command Center to keep the economy humming.

[Step 8] Research Combat Shield while pumping out Marines, add two depots to avoid supply blocks, keep SCV production steady, and start the first Medivac for early drops or reinforcement mobility.

[Step 9] Take the natural expansion while keeping SCV production going, throw down a couple of depots to avoid getting supply blocked, and start a second Barracks to ramp up marine production.

[Step 10] Start reinforcing your army by dropping two additional raxes and adding a reactor to one of them for double marine production. Continue training a mix of marauders and marines from all raxes, while keeping your starport busy producing a medivac and a viking for medivac support and air control.

[Step 11] Drop a second Barracks, then start a Marauder from the first while adding a Depot to manage supply. Attach a Reactor to the new Barracks to pump out several Marines, put a Reactor on the Starport for double production, and throw down two Refineries to secure the gas income needed for tech transitions.

[Step 12] Morph the Command Center into an Orbital Command for the extra energy and supply, then start pushing out the first wave of infantry with two Marauders backed by half a dozen Marines to apply early pressure or defend your position.

[Step 13] Produce a pair of Marauders and several Marines from the Barracks, while adding a couple of Medivacs from the Starport to provide healing and dropship support. Throw down a couple of Depots to keep supply open as you reinforce the bio ball.

[Step 14] Add a couple of depots to keep supply flowing while pumping out a solid bio force from the rax, training six marines and a marauder to set up for an early timing attack.

[Step 15] Keep pumping out medivacs and marauders from your production, reinforce with a wave of marines, and throw down an extra depot to avoid getting supply blocked.

[Step 16] From your rax, produce two Marauders along with several Marines, and throw down a couple of depots to prevent supply blocks.

[Step 17] Keep the rax pumping nonstop — train a few rounds of Marines and mix in some Marauders for extra damage, while adding several depots to avoid getting supply blocked as your army swells.

[Step 18] Maintain constant production of Marines, Marauders, and Medivacs from your five Rax and two Starports while dropping depots and taking a fourth base—if you scout heavy baneling or archon play, swap in more Marauders and spread your tanks, and if the opponent masses air, add extra Vikings and switch one Starport to Tech Lab for Ravens or Liberators. As your third base saturates, transition into Ghosts for EMP against Protoss or for snipe versus high-tech Zerg, then eventually add Liberators for zone control on the map. Use this gameplan as your strategic baseline — adapt your decisions based on what you scout and how the game unfolds.
