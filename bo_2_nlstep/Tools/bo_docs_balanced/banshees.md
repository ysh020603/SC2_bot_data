# Summary

Drop a depot and rax with your first gas, then fast expand with a second CC and grab a factory into starport with a tech lab, dropping a bunker for security while you tech up to a banshee and siege tank. Next you double up on raxes and add a second starport, grabbing combat shield while cranking out a marine-tank-banshee combo to harass and secure a third CC. Eventually you drop three more raxes and a fourth CC to sustain a maxed-out bio-tank-banshee timing that pressures from all angles.

# Details

[Step 1] Start the game by building a supply depot at your natural ramp, then queue up SCV production from the Command Center. After the first couple of workers, drop your first Barracks and a Refinery to begin gas collection, then keep SCV production steady with a few more workers before adding a second depot to avoid supply blocking.

[Step 2] Morph the first Command Center into an Orbital Command, then take a natural expansion with a new CC. Drop a Refinery and start the Factory for tech transition while constantly producing several SCVs and a few Marines for early defense, adding a Supply Depot to keep up with production.

[Step 3] Start the Starport with a Tech Lab ready to come online, upgrade the main CC to an Orbital Command for extra income and scans, and drop a Bunker for early defense. Maintain steady worker and Marine production while adding a second Barracks and attaching a Tech Lab to the Factory to push toward more advanced technology.

[Step 4] Keep workers coming from the Command Center and add a depot to stay safe on supply, while reinforcing with a couple of Marines and producing a Siege Tank and a Banshee to apply pressure and secure map control.

[Step 5] Continue worker production while training a wave of Marines and throwing down a couple of depots to keep supply in check. Start the first Siege Tank from the factory to add some early siege power for defense or a timing push.

[Step 6] Continue worker production while adding a second Barracks and a second Starport to your production line. Meanwhile, have your existing Starport start training a Banshee for early harassment, and attach a Tech Lab to one Barracks and a Reactor to the other. Don't forget to grab another Refinery to secure your gas income for upcoming tech transitions.

[Step 7] Keep worker production steady while reinforcing with both a Siege Tank and a Banshee, training a couple of Marines for defense. Add multiple depots to avoid supply blocks, take a fourth Refinery to support gas-heavy tech, and attach a Tech Lab to the Starport for upgrades or cloak.

[Step 8] Start researching Combat Shield at the Barracks while pumping out a wave of Marines, then begin building a Banshee from the Starport for early harassment. To keep supply from capping, throw down a couple of depots, and reinforce with a few more Marines to maintain pressure or defense.

[Step 9] Add two depots to avoid supply blocks while producing a few marines, then follow up with a Siege Tank and a Banshee for a strong mid-game timing. After that, reinforce with a wave of marines to round out the army.

[Step 10] Continue pumping Marines and throwing down supply depots to keep from getting blocked, while starting a Banshee out of the Starport for harassment and a Siege Tank from the Factory for early firepower and map control.

[Step 11] Pump out a wave of Marines from the rax while the Starport produces a Banshee for harassing the opponent's mineral line, and throw down a couple of depots to keep the supply flowing.

[Step 12] Pump out a Siege Tank from the Factory followed by a Banshee from the Starport while adding several depots to keep supply capped. Crank out four Marines for ground support and a second Banshee to strengthen the harass or defense.

[Step 13] Keep up with supply by adding a few depots, then start pumping out a wave of Marines. Transition smoothly by building a Siege Tank from the Factory, then add more depots before pulling a Banshee from the Starport to apply early pressure while continuing Marine production.

[Step 14] Add another rax and a couple of depots to keep up with supply, then pump out a handful of Marines along with a Siege Tank and a pair of Banshees to start mounting pressure.

[Step 15] Drop an additional Command Center for your next expansion while adding two more Barracks to ramp up bio production. Train several Marines, a couple of Siege Tanks, and a handful of Banshees to form a versatile mid-game army. Don't forget to throw down a couple of Supply Depots to maintain your population.

[Step 16] Keep producing marines, siege tanks, and banshees from your 5 raxes, factory, and 2 starports while constantly adding SCVs and depots to sustain a maxed army; if you scout heavy air like mutas or colossi, swap some banshees for vikings or thors, and if you see a fast third base from the opponent, drop a few more tanks to lock down their expansions; as you secure a fourth base and approach 200 supply, consider adding a fusion core for battlecruisers to break entrenched positions or upgrade to +3/+3 for a decisive push; use this gameplan as your strategic baseline — adapt your decisions based on what you scout and how the game unfolds.
