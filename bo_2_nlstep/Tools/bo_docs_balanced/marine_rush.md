# Summary

Open with a standard three-rax on one base, skip the expansion and gas to pump pure marines with constant depots and OC snipes. Crank out nonstop marines from triple rax and stay on one base for a relentless early-mid pressure, never bothering with stim or combat shield since you're all-inning on lings. Max out on pure marines and march across the map to overwhelm the opponent with sheer numbers, aiming to end the game before any tech or expansions matter.

# Details

[Step 1] Maintain constant SCV production from the Command Center, drop a Supply Depot to avoid a supply block, then start the first Barracks. Follow up with a second depot while adding two more Barracks to establish a solid three-rax opener.

[Step 2] Morph the Command Center into an Orbital, add the second and third rax, and keep pumping out Marines along with a SCV to set up a strong early-game pressure.

[Step 3] Throw down another depot to keep supply ahead, continue worker production, then add the second Barracks and start pumping out a wave of Marines to build up a solid early army.

[Step 4] Pump out several Marines from the rax while throwing down depots to avoid supply blocks, reinforcing your army to prepare for early pressure or a quick tech transition.

[Step 5] Pump out a wave of 9 marines from your rax and add a depot to keep up with the growing army supply.

[Step 6] Train 10 Marines from the Barracks to build up your early army count.

[Step 7] Pump out a wave of Marines from the rax and throw down an extra depot to keep your supply from capping out.

[Step 8] Throw down three more depots to free up supply, and start massing a wave of Marines from the rax to reinforce your army.

[Step 9] Crank out a large wave of Marines from your Barracks to build up your army, and throw down an additional Supply Depot to keep the production flowing.

[Step 10] Keep pumping marines from all three barracks while using your Orbital Command's energy for supply drops or scans to spot the enemy army, and if your initial wave fails to break them, consider adding a factory for widow mines or a quick expansion to sustain a longer fight, remembering that against heavy banelings you should spread your marines before engaging, or if you scout a bunker at their natural, you can pull SCVs for a decisive repair and shield your push; use this gameplan as your strategic baseline — adapt your decisions based on what you scout and how the game unfolds.
