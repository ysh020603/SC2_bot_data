# Summary

Fast expand into an early third CC while teching to a Factory with a Tech Lab for Cyclones and researching Blue Flame for Hellions, using a single Marine for scouting and constant worker production. Pump out five Factories with reactors and tech labs, cranking out Hellions and Cyclones while slapping down an Engineering Bay and Armories for +1 vehicle weapons, aiming for a relentless mech mid-game pressure on three bases. Spam Armories for max upgrades and mass Cyclone-Hellion armies, aiming to overwhelm with sustained production and early timing pushes on a three-base economy.

Cap off this phase by dropping a new Command Center to expand your economy.

[Step 9] Start churning out a couple of Cyclones for early pressure or defense while throwing down several depots to keep supply from capping, and keep SCV production humming to maintain a solid economy for your mech push.
