# Summary

The build opens with a standard depot-into-rax setup, grabbing a quick natural expansion and transitioning into Factory tech with a tech lab to unlock Siege Tank production, while keeping a couple of marines around for early defense and securing the natural. Production scales hard with a massive 8-rax drop alongside a 3-base economy, stacking reactors on the rax to flood marines while keeping 2-3 Factories pumping tanks, eventually hitting a critical marine-tank ball with combat shield done and a siege line ready to move. The endgame plan is to max out on a pure marine-tank composition and hit a decisive timing push, using the bio ball to buffer for the tanks and overwhelm with sheer production volume rather than tech switching or going sky terran.
