# Summary

The build opens with a standard depot-into-rax setup, getting gas online quickly and morphing the CC into an orbital before adding a factory and a starport, with reapers and hellions providing early map presence while a raven comes out for detection and the natural expansion secures the economic foundation. Production scales hard with a cluster of rax added alongside tech labs and reactors, pumping out a bio-mechanical core of marines, tanks, and vikings while stim, combat shields, and bio upgrades kick in at the ebays, supported by medivacs and liberators from the starport for a sustained pressure playstyle that keeps the opponent on the back foot. The economy powers a 3-base mineral line and gas-heavy tech demands, with the armory unlocking mech upgrades to push the tank and viking core into a maxed-out bio-tank deathball that aims to siege across the map with overwhelming production and upgraded infantry anchoring the frontline.

[Step 2] Morph the CC into an orbital, then throw down a factory and a depot to keep supply smooth. Train a couple of reapers for early map pressure and scout denial while keeping worker production rolling. Add a factory and churn out a pair of hellions for harassment or defense, then secure another CC to set up the economic backbone for the midgame.

[Step 3] Throw down a starport and add tech labs to both the factory and rax, then queue up a cyclone and a tank from the factory while training a raven from the port for early detection and harassment defense. Keep worker production rolling with several SCVs, morph an OC, and add another refinery to support the growing tech demands.

[Step 4] Take an expansion while keeping worker production rolling and add a couple of depots to stay ahead of supply. Queue up a raven from the starport for detection and map control, then slap a reactor onto a rax to start doubling marine output.

[Step 5] Keep worker production rolling with several SCVs while throwing down a couple of depots to stay ahead of supply. Add another rax for more bio production, queue up a tank from the factory, and start a viking from the starport to begin establishing air control and scout for incoming threats.

[Step 6] Add 3 rax to support a heavier bio footprint while keeping worker production steady, morph an OC for macro boost, and build another tank from the factory. Queue up a pair of vikings from the starport to secure air control and spot for incoming threats.
