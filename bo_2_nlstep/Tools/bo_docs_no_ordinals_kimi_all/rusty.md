# Summary

The opening is a standard rax-first into fast expand with an early orbital and a quick follow-up CC, transitioning immediately into a Factory with a tech lab to start tank production while keeping marine count low for defense. The mid-game powers up with 3 rax, a Starport, and a another Factory to flood bio-mech, grabbing combat shields and mixing tech labs with reactors to pump marines, tanks, and medivacs for a heavy sustained pressure playstyle. The late-game aims to max out on a bio-mech core with siege tank anchor, medivac support, and viking sprinkle, keeping the economy rolling across multiple bases to remax quickly and overwhelm with endless marine waves backed by armored punch.

# Details

[Step 1] Keep worker production rolling while throwing down a depot, a refinery, and a rax to establish early gas and production. Add another depot once the rax is up, then train a marine to start building a small defense force.

[Step 2] Morph the CC into an orbital and immediately take another CC for a fast expansion, keeping worker production rolling the whole time. Throw down a Refinery to start gas income, then add a Factory with a tech lab to move into tank production. Train a couple of Marines for early defense and add a depot to keep supply ahead of the curve.

[Step 3] Morph the CC into an orbital to unlock mule production and scouting, then add another factory alongside the existing one to ramp up tank output. Keep workers flowing and reinforce with a few marines while 2 more tanks roll off the production line, establishing a solid defensive core before pushing out.

[Step 4] Throw down 2 refineries to ramp up gas income while keeping worker production rolling with several SCVs. Add a factory tech lab and start a tank, then drop a depot to keep supply ahead of the growing production.

[Step 5] Keep workers coming non-stop while throwing down a starport and building 3 rax to power up bio production. Drop a couple depots to stay ahead of supply and smooth out the transition into medivac-supported infantry play.

[Step 6] Add another rax with a pair of tech labs to support the bio core, then queue up a medivac and 2 tanks from the existing factory and starport to round out the composition. Grab combat shields for the bio, and reinforce with a few marines to keep the pressure on.

[Step 7] Throw down a cluster of depots to clear supply block while adding reactors to the rax and starport to flood out units. Queue up a couple of tanks from the factory and train a marine, setting up for a heavy bio-mech production cycle. The goal here is to smooth out macro and get multiple tech labs and reactors online so you can remax quickly.

[Step 8] Take another CC to secure a another base while keeping marine production rolling and throwing down a couple of depots to stay ahead of supply. Build 2 Siege Tanks from the factory, queue up a medivac and a viking from the starport, and train another marine to keep the bio numbers up—this sets up a solid mid-game push with tank support and air coverage.

[Step 9] Pump out a wave of Marines from the rax while queuing up a Medivac at the starport, then reinforce with a couple of Tanks from the factory to round out a solid bio-mech core for the mid-game push.

[Step 10] Push out a medivac and start massing marines hard off the rax, reinforcing with another medivac shortly after while the marine flood continues to build up for the upcoming timing attack.

[Step 11] Morph a CC into an orbital to keep the macro cycle rolling, churn out 2 tanks from the factory to anchor your position, queue up a medivac for eventual drops or pickup support, and flood out 6 marines to reinforce your bio numbers and prepare for the upcoming push.

[Step 12] Keep marine production rolling nonstop while adding tanks from the factory to build a strong bio-meath core, then throw down a depot to stay ahead on supply.

[Step 13] Throw down 3 depots to shore up supply while massing out 6 Marines and producing a Siege Tank, building up a solid bio-meek core to prepare for the mid-game push.

[Step 14] Add a viking and another tank to the army while throwing down depots to keep supply ahead of the production curve. Train a wave of marines from the rax to reinforce the bio core, then drop a couple more depots so you don't get supply blocked as the army grows.

[Step 15] Pump out a wave of Marines from your rax while producing 2 Siege Tanks from the factory, building up a solid bio-meek core to prepare for the mid-game push.

[Step 16] Flood out a massive wave of Marines from all your rax while sneaking in a Siege Tank from the factory to give your bio ball some real punch against armored targets.

[Step 17] Throw down 4 depots to clear supply block and make room for a massive marine production wave, churning out 10 marines while adding 1 tank from the factory and a viking from the starport to round out the army composition.

[Step 18] This is a classic bio-mech macro build that leans on a fast expand into heavy sustained production, flooding marines and siege tanks with medivac support to create an overwhelming, remax-heavy pressure style that grinds opponents down through relentless waves. The tempo is macro-oriented but with a clear mid-game power spike, using the tank anchor to force engagements while the bio core swarms and trades efficiently. Use this gameplan as your strategic baseline -- adapt your decisions based on what you scout and how the game unfolds.
