# Summary

The opening rolls out a standard rax-first into quick CC expansion, grabbing an early refinery to power Factory-Starport tech while dropping a bunker for safety and morphing both CCs into orbitals to keep the macro flowing. Production scales through 3 rax with mixed tech labs and reactors, 2 ports cranking Banshees, and a Factory pumping Tanks, but notably skips stim in favor of combat shields while leaning on a bio-meath core supported by air harass. The endgame aims to flood the map with a maxed-out mixed army of Marines, Banshees, and Siege Tanks, taking a 3-base economy and overwhelming with sustained production rather than chasing a specific late-tech switch.
