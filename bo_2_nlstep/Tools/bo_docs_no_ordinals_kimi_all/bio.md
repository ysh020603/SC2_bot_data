# Summary

Rusty Infantry opens with a standard rax-first into quick expansion, getting gas early for a reaper scout before transitioning into a factory and eventually twin starports while dropping an OC on the natural. The build scales into a heavy bio setup with 5+ rax and double port production, grabbing stim, combat shield, and concussive shells to power a classic MMM ball backed by tank support for a strong two-base timing. The endgame plan is to hit a critical mass bio timing off 3 bases with medivac reinforcement, aiming to crack the opponent before needing a major tech switch or late-game transition.

[Step 1] Keep worker production rolling while throwing down a depot, then add 2 rax and a refinery to open up gas. Once the gas is flowing, churn out a reaper for early map presence and scouting.

[Step 2] Morph the CC into an orbital for scan and mule economy, then grab the natural expansion while keeping a few SCVs in production. Throw down a depot and add a reactor to the rax for double marine output, then toss up a factory to start transitioning into factory tech.

[Step 3] Keep marines and SCVs rolling out while adding a factory tech lab for tech progression, then throw down 4 depots to clear supply block and prepare for heavier production.

[Step 4] Morph the CC into an orbital to unlock mule production and scouting, then throw down another refinery to fuel the factory. Keep worker production rolling with a couple of SCVs while pumping out a tank and several marines, then add a tech lab to the rax for stim or combat shield access.

[Step 5] Add a starport and a rax while keeping worker production rolling, then drop another starport to set up double port production. Grab concussive shells for your marauders and crank out a few marines to hold the fort while the tech comes online.

[Step 6] Keep worker production rolling while throwing down a couple of depots to stay ahead of supply. Add a tech lab to the rax and start mixing in a marauder alongside a few marines, while the factory pumps out another tank to keep the mech core growing.

[Step 7] Kick off stim research at the rax tech lab while keeping marine production rolling. Throw down a couple depots to clear supply block, then churn out several more SCVs to keep the economy humming.

[Step 8] Start combat shield research while keeping marine production rolling and throwing down a couple depots to stay ahead of supply. Keep workers training nonstop and queue up a medivac to prepare for bio drop play or field reinforcement.

[Step 9] Train a couple of Marines and keep SCV production rolling while throwing down 3 depots to clear the supply block, then take an expansion and add another rax to ramp up bio production.

[Step 10] Drop 2 rax to crank up bio output while adding a reactor to one of them, then queue a medivac and a viking from the starport to round out the army comp. Train a mix of marauders and marines from the new and existing rax—aim for a solid bio ball with a few marauders up front and marines filling out the backline.

[Step 11] Add another rax and throw down a depot to keep supply ahead of production, then slap a reactor on the new rax and start cranking out Marines while a single Marauder trains up for some early punch. [Step 12] Morph the CC into an orbital to unlock mule production and scouting scans, then power out a wave of bio with 2 marauders followed by 7 marines to build a strong two-base timing push.

[Step 13] Reinforce the army with a few marauders and a wave of marines while adding a pair of medivacs to the production queue; throw down a couple depots to keep supply ahead of the growing bio force.

[Step 14] Throw down a depot and keep the rax busy with a marauder followed by a wave of marines to build up a solid bio ball. Add another couple of depots to stay ahead on supply as the army grows.

[Step 15] Queue up 2 medivacs and 2 marauders from the starport and rax, then reinforce with a wave of 5 marines while throwing down a depot to keep supply flowing for the upcoming push.

[Step 16] Flood the rax with production, churning out 2 marauders and 6 marines to bulk up your bio force, while throwing down 2 depots to keep supply from capping as you mass up for the push.

[Step 17] Push out a wave of bio with 2 marauders and a handful of marines, then throw down 4 depots to keep supply ahead of the production curve. Keep the rax churning with another 8 marines and 2 more marauders to build a critical mass for an upcoming timing push.

[Step 18] This is a classic MMM bio snowball that leverages sustained rax production and double-starport medivac reinforcement to hit a crushing mid-game timing off a solid three-base economy.
