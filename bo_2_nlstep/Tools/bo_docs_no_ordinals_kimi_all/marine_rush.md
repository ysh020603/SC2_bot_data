# Summary

This build opens with a heavy rax-first commit, skipping both the natural CC and gas entirely to slam down a cluster of rax off a single mineral line while keeping worker production rolling. The mid-game is pure marine flood with no tech labs, no upgrades, and no factory or starport in sight—just depots and rax stacked to the ceiling as the bot tries to drown the opponent in bio before any real tech comes online. The late-game plan is essentially a prayer that the marine ball cracks the opponent before they get splash, since there is no economy beyond one base, no transition path, and no answer to banelings, colossus, or storms—just a maxed-out timing push that lives or dies on whether sheer marine count can brute-force a win.

# Details

[Step 1] Keep worker production rolling and throw down a couple of depots to avoid supply blocks, then build 3 rax to establish a solid bio production backbone.

[Step 2] Morph the main CC into an orbital while keeping worker production rolling, then throw down 2 rax to support heavy marine production. Train a wave of 6 marines out of the existing and new rax to build up early pressure and defend against any aggression.

[Step 3] Throw down a depot to keep supply ahead while training a wave of Marines and adding another rax for extra production. Keep workers coming and use the Marine numbers to apply pressure or hold the natural against early aggression.

[Step 4] Keep the rax pumping marines non-stop to build up a solid bio force, and throw down a couple of depots to stay ahead of supply as the army grows.

[Step 5] Keep rax production rolling and pump out a wave of Marines while throwing down a depot to avoid supply block.

[Step 6] Flood out a massive wave of Marines from the rax to build up a strong bio force, preparing for an aggressive push or to secure map control with sheer numbers.

[Step 7] Push out a wave of Marines from the rax to build a critical mass of bio, then throw down a depot to stay ahead of supply as the army grows.

[Step 8] Throw down 3 depots to clear supply block and make room for a massive marine production cycle, then queue up 7 marines across your rax to flood the field with bio.

[Step 9] Dump minerals into a massive wave of Marines from your rax, churning out around a dozen to flood the field, and throw down a depot to keep supply from choking as the army swells.

[Step 10] This is a pure all-in one-base marine flood that sacrifices all economy, tech, and transition potential for a single-minded bio swarm timing—classic "Oops All Marines" cheese aggression that lives or dies on brute-force saturation. Use this gameplan as your strategic baseline -- adapt your decisions based on what you scout and how the game unfolds.
