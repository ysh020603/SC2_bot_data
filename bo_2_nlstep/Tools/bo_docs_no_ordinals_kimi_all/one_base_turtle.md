# Summary

The opening is a standard rax-first into quick orbital with a fast factory follow-up, but notably skips the natural CC entirely to commit to a one-base turtle setup that throws down bunkers and a tech-labbed factory to start stockpiling siege tanks for an early defensive shell. Production scales through adding 2 more rax for marine flood alongside the tank factory, running a pure bio-mech reinforcement pattern with no starport or tech upgrades like stim, leaning entirely on raw unit count and siege positioning to control space rather than mobility or harassment. The endgame plan caps out at a maxed one-base bio-tank deathball that aims to break the opponent with overwhelming marine-tank production and siege pressure, though without an expansion or late-game tech switches the build runs out of gas and has no transition beyond the initial timing push.
