# Summary

The opening is a standard rax-first into expand with an early orbital, grabbing gas to support a Factory and then a quick Starport while shoringing up with a bunker and a few marines for defense. Production scales through a 3-rax setup with mixed add-ons, combat shield timing, and a Fusion Core to unlock BC tech, running a bio-mech core of marines and siege tanks while the starport cranks out battlecruisers for a heavy air transition. The late-game direction is a massive marine-tank-BC deathball backed by a sprawling multi-base economy with tons of orbitals for mule and scan energy, aiming to overwhelm with sustained production and a sky-terran fleet that forces the opponent into an unwinnable engagement.
