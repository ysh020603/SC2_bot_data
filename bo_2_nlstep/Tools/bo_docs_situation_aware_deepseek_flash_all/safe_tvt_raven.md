# Summary

Open with a gas-first rax into factory and starport, taking a CC on the low ground after Reaper-Hellion pressure, then adding a Raven for early detection and harass. Midgame you mass marines off a reactor rax while adding tanks and Vikings from the factory and starport, getting double ebay upgrades, stim, and combat shield to support a bio-mech push. Lategame you saturate 3 bases with 7 rax and a tech-labbed factory, cranking out marines, tanks, and Vikings for a relentless siege-style composition backed by vehicle and infantry upgrades.
