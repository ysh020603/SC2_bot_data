# Summary

We open rax-gas-rax for a Reaper into fast double Orbital, dropping a natural CC early while teching to Factory for a Siege Tank and adding a tech lab on the Barracks for Marauder access. Mid-game we slam down a bunch of extra rax with reactors, pump bio with Stim and Combat Shield, and mix in Medivacs and a Viking for map control while saturating another. Late game we keep slamming depots and pumping Marauder-heavy bio off a 3-base economy, using constant Medivac production to sustain aggressive pressure until we max out and roll over the opponent.
