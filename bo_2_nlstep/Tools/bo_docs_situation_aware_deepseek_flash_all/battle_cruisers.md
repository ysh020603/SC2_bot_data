# Summary

The build opens depot-first into a standard rax and gas, then drops a quick CC for an expansion while getting an Orbital and a Factory, followed by a Starport and a Bunker for early defense. Mid-game sees the player adding a Tech Lab on the Factory for Tanks, a Fusion Core for BC tech, and a reactor on another rax, massing Marines and Tanks while pumping out Battlecruisers for a gas-heavy core. Late-game, the economy supports three bases with multiple OCs, the army is a mix of BCs, Tanks, and Marines with Combat Shield, and the plan is to overwhelm the opponent with sustained bio-mech production and constant BC reinforcement.
