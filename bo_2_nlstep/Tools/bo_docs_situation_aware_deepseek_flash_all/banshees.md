# Summary

Open depot-first into a quick Rax and gas, then drop an early expansion and Orbital Command while adding a Factory and Starport to set up a gas-heavy tech path with a Bunker for defense. Mid-game scales with a Reactor on the Rax and Tech Labs on both the Factory and Starport, cranking out Marines, Siege Tanks, and Banshees with Combat Shield to apply mobile harassment and positional pressure while saturating the economy. Late-game adds two more Rax and a fresh Command Center for another, aiming for sustained bio-mech and air production with an overwhelming mix of Marines, Tanks, and Banshees to max out and push.
