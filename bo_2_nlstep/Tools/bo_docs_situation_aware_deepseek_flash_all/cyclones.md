# Summary

The build opens with a depot-first rax and quick gas to get the ball rolling, dropping a factory for early Cyclone production and a fast expansion before upgrading to orbitals for MULEs and scans. Mid-game sees 3+ factories pumping mass Cyclones and Hellions for mobile map presence, with tech labs and a reactor for flexible production, while an Engineering Bay starts vehicle weapons upgrades and a Planetary Fortress secures another. Late-game cranks up to a heavy mech deathball of Cyclones and Hellions supported by multiple Armories for upgrades, with a saturated 3-base economy feeding sustained production and constant pressure.

Drop a Refinery to secure gas income and prepare for tech later, then add another depot to stay ahead of supply as SCVs and a marine queue up.

[Step 2] Morph both Command Centers into Orbitals to start generating MULEs and scans, while keeping SCV production steady to saturate the mineral lines. Drop a Factory for tech progression and a Refinery to build up gas income for future mech or air upgrades, add a few Marines from the Rax for early defense, and make sure you have enough depot room to support the growing worker count and unit production.

[Step 3] Keep SCV production humming to saturate the mineral lines while dropping a pair of Refineries to secure the gas economy for the factory tech, add a tech lab to the factory, start training a Cyclone, and take a new Command Center to expand your economy.

[Step 4] Drop a Factory and a Cyclone to start building a mech presence, while keeping worker production steady and throwing down depots to maintain supply headroom for the growing economy and unit count.

Morph the new Command Center into an Orbital Command and throw down a Refinery to strengthen gas income for the upcoming tech path.
