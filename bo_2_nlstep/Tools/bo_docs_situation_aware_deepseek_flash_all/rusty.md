# Summary

The opening is a standard 1-1-1 with a depot-first, a fast gas at the natural, and a Factory with a Tech Lab to start Siege Tank production while taking an early expansion CC and getting an Orbital Command. Mid-game scales with another Factory, a Starport with a Reactor for Medivacs and Vikings, and a pair of Tech Lab Rax to pump Marines and start Combat Shields, creating a Tank-heavy bio-mech push with solid map presence. Late-game aims for a sustained, gas-heavy mech composition supported by another base, cranking out Marines, Tanks, Medivacs, and Vikings to overwhelm the opponent with constant production and siege power.
