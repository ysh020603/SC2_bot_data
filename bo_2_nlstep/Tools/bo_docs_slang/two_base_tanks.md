# Summary

This build goes for a fast expand, grabbing the natural early while dropping a gas and factory for quick siege tanks, then pumps heavy marine production from a ton of rax—reactored for speed—while the factory keeps churning out tanks; the core idea is a two-base bio-mech timing, using tanks for siege defense and marines for bulk, with OCs for mules, and eventually adding a third CC to secure the eco for the big push.

# Details

[Step 1] Start by sending an SCV to build the first depot immediately, then keep pumping SCVs from the Command Center nonstop. As minerals allow, drop the first Barracks and a Refinery to set up early production and gas income.

[Step 2] Expand to the natural and throw down a depot, then produce a couple SCVs and a marine before adding a Refinery and starting a Factory, all while keeping up worker and marine production.

[Step 3] Keep SCV production going throughout this phase—build four workers—while throwing down several Supply Depots to maintain supply. Attach a Tech Lab to your existing Factory for tech access, then start construction on a second Factory to ramp up mech production.

[Step 4] Keep pumping SCVs from the Command Center while the Factory produces a Siege Tank, then add a Tech Lab on the Factory for future upgrades and throw down an extra Supply Depot to maintain headroom.

[Step 5] Drop the first Refinery and start producing a Siege Tank from the Factory, while constantly training workers and throwing down several depots to keep supply open for this tech transition.

[Step 6] Keep SCV production humming while adding the second Barracks with a Tech Lab, start researching Combat Shield, produce a couple of Siege Tanks from the Factory, and drop a third Refinery to secure extra gas for the upcoming tech.

[Step 7] Morph both Command Centers into Orbital Commands for added scan and mule potential, then pump out a few Siege Tanks from the Factory and a wave of Marines from the Barracks—roughly 4 tanks and 6 marines—while adding a couple of depots to keep supply from capping.

[Step 8] Drop a wave of 3 Barracks to kick off your production ramp, then add a pair of Supply Depots to keep your supply from capping, and finish by putting down 5 more Barracks—getting you a huge marine-producing machine for a big timing push.

[Step 9] Add two more Barracks and fit them with Reactors to boost Marine production while the Factory pumps out several Siege Tanks to support a sturdy bio-mech composition.

[Step 10] Drop your first Factory and start producing a pair of Siege Tanks while pumping out a steady stream of Marines from the rax, building a balanced bio-mech force to hold your ground and prepare for an early push.

[Step 11] Keep churning out Marines from the Barracks while adding a Tech Lab to the Factory for upcoming tech upgrades. Also throw down several Supply Depots to prevent any supply block as the army expands.

[Step 12] Drop a full wall of 10 supply depots to stay well ahead on supply and avoid any blockages during your upcoming production and tech transitions.

[Step 13] Throw down a depot to keep supply open while pumping out three Siege Tanks from the Factory and reinforcing with several Marines from the Barracks to build up a solid mech bio force.

[Step 14] Pump out a wave of Marines from the rax while the factory produces your first Siege Tank to set up a solid early push.

[Step 15] Ramp up production from the factory and rax — train 2 Siege Tanks for siege mobility and a handful of Marines to bulk up the bio line, keeping your army supply high and ready to apply pressure.

[Step 16] Pump out a wave of marines from the rax while adding a tank from the factory to reinforce the army with both bio and siege support.

[Step 17] Pump out a wave of Marines from your Barracks to reinforce your army, training 11 of them to strengthen your push or defend against incoming aggression.

[Step 18] Take your natural expansion with a new Command Center while morphing your main base's Command Center into an Orbital Command, then crank out a wave of Marines from your Barracks to build up your army count.
