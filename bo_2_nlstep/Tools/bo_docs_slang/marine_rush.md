# Summary

Mass marines off a 3-rax opener—drop the first rax after depot, then slam down two more rax for the bio core, morph the CC to OC for faster gas-free income, and just non-stop pump marines while spamming depots to avoid getting supply blocked. Pure marine aggression with no tech or gas investment; you’re looking to overwhelm the opponent with a constant stream of infantry right out the gate, keeping the pressure on while never stopping worker production.

# Details

[Step 1] Kick off the game by maintaining constant SCV production while throwing down an initial depot, then place the first Barracks. After adding a second depot, continue training workers as you drop the second and third Barracks in rapid succession, building a solid 3-rax foundation for your early army.

[Step 2] Morph the first Command Center into an Orbital Command for faster income, then drop two additional Barracks to set up a 3-rax bio opener, while continuously producing SCVs and pumping out a steady stream of Marines for early pressure and defense.

[Step 3] Add a supply depot to keep up with the growing army, while continuing to pump SCVs from the Command Center. Drop a second Barracks and start massing Marines to build a solid infantry core for early map presence or to defend against early aggression.

[Step 4] Continue churning out Marines from the Barracks while throwing down additional depots to prevent a supply block—keep the infantry count climbing steadily.

[Step 5] Pump out a wave of Marines from the Barracks to build up a solid bio force, and throw down a Supply Depot to keep your supply from capping out.

[Step 6] Crank out a wave of 10 Marines from the Barracks to build a strong bio force for the next engagement.

[Step 7] Crank out a wave of marines from the rax to reinforce your army, then throw down another depot to keep supply capped.

[Step 8] Throw down three more depots to avoid supply block, then start churning out a round of 7 Marines from the Barracks to bolster your early army.

[Step 9] Pump out a sizable wave of Marines from the rax to bulk up your army, and throw down an extra depot to keep supply flowing for the next production cycle.
