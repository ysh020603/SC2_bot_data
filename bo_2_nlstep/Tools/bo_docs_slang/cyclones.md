# Summary

Open with a fast expand—CC natural before even a rax—then get both OCs up and rush a Factory for early Cyclones while grabbing a second refinery. Tech straight into Cyclone Lock-On and blue flame for Hellions, throwing down multiple Factories to mass a mobile mech army of Cyclones and Hellions for map control and harassment. Grab your third base, drop an EBay for upgrades, and eventually fortify your nat with a PF. As you saturate, keep adding Factories, tech labs, and armories to push vehicle weapons upgrades, aiming to overwhelm with a relentless wave of Hellion/Cyclone pressure.

# Details

[Step 1] Start by throwing down a Supply Depot and keep SCV production humming, then take your natural expansion with a new Command Center. Follow up with your first Barracks and a Refinery, rallying a Marine for early defense as you settle into the macro rhythm.

[Step 2] Morph both Command Centers into Orbital Commands for extra scanning and MULEs, then throw down a Factory to start tech transitions while adding a Refinery for gas. Keep worker production going at all bases and train a couple of Marines to reinforce the early army.

[Step 3] Keep SCV production flowing while training a Marine for defense, add a Tech Lab to the Factory for tech access, drop two Refineries to boost gas income, and take a new expansion with a Command Center; finish the phase by producing a Cyclone for early map control.

[Step 4] Begin the first Factory to transition into mech, add a couple of depots and keep SCVs flowing from the Command Center. As soon as the Factory finishes, start producing a Cyclone for early pressure or defense.

[Step 5] Continue pumping out workers while adding a Tech Lab onto the Factory. Research the Cyclone Lock-On Damage upgrade and produce a couple of Cyclones. Morph the current Command Center into an Orbital Command, and grab a third Refinery. Meanwhile, start the Infernal Pre-Igniter upgrade (blue flame) for Hellions, and keep training SCVs to maintain a solid economy.

[Step 6] Drop the first Factory, then start pumping out workers from the main while the Factory completes—produce a Hellion for early harass or map control. As soon as the first Factory finishes, throw down a second Factory to ramp up mech production, and start building a Cyclone for immediate pressure while keeping worker production steady.

[Step 7] Throw down several depots to avoid supply blockage, keep worker production steady, and start building the first Cyclone out of the Factory.

[Step 8] Throw down two depots and keep SCV production going, then start a Cyclone while adding a reactor and a tech lab to the factory. Follow up with taking your third base by building a new Command Center.

[Step 9] Continue worker production from the main base, pump out a couple of Cyclones for early map control or aggression, and add multiple depots to maintain supply as you expand your factory output.

[Step 10] Continue worker production while dropping four Supply Depots to prevent supply blocks. Set up an Engineering Bay for upgrades and a Refinery for gas income, then begin building a Cyclone for early map control or defense.

[Step 11] Keep SCV production steady while dropping a couple of depots to stay ahead on supply. From the factory, start pumping out a few Hellions and a couple of Cyclones to bolster your early mech army.

[Step 12] Keep SCV production going while adding a depot to avoid supply block. From the factory, pump out several Cyclones and a couple of Hellions to build up an aggressive mech army. Also take two additional refineries for gas income, and at the natural expansion, upgrade the Command Center into a Planetary Fortress to fortify your position.

[Step 13] Add several more Factories and begin producing a mix of Hellions and Cyclones to build a mobile mech force for map control and early harassment.

[Step 14] Build your first Factory and immediately start a Cyclone, then add two more Factories to support a heavy mech composition. Produce several Cyclones and a couple of Hellions for map control and harassment, and keep up with supply by throwing down an extra depot or two.

[Step 15] Pump out several Hellions from the Factory while adding tech labs to enable further upgrades, and throw down a few extra depots to keep supply flowing.

[Step 16] Reinforce with a couple of Hellions while adding two Tech Labs to the Factories for advanced mech production, train several Cyclones for mobility and damage, and throw down two Supply Depots to avoid getting supply blocked.

[Step 17] Drop 4 armories to ramp up mech upgrades, produce a Hellion for map control, follow up with a Cyclone for added firepower, and throw down several depots to keep supply ahead.

[Step 18] Reinforce with two Cyclones while adding multiple Supply Depots to stay ahead on supply as you prepare for the next tech or expansion.

[Step 19] Pump out a couple of Cyclones and a wave of Hellions from the factories while starting vehicle weapons upgrade to boost your mech army's damage output.
