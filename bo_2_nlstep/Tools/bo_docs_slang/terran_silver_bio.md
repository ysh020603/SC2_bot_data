# Summary

This is a macro bio opening with a fast expand—drops a rax and gas first, then a second rax and gas before taking nat CC, going for early reaper harassment and concussive shells to keep the bio army sticky. Tech path is pure barracks: tech lab on first rax, reactor on second, then mass marine–marauder with combat shield, no factory or starport at all. After nat is up, it adds two more rax and eventually reactors on all four, securing extra refineries to gas up marauder production, and spams constant marine waves for a strong two-base bio timing push. Final direction is relentless marine-marauder all-in off two CCs.

# Details

[Step 1] Start the opening by dropping a Supply Depot and training a couple of SCVs, then throw down the first Barracks and a Refinery for early gas intake. Keep worker production going with two more SCVs while adding a second Rax and a second Refinery to support a fast tech or heavy bio follow‑up.

[Step 2] Morph your first Command Center into an Orbital Command, then start a Reaper for early harassment while training a couple of Marines and keeping SCV production going. Next, throw down your natural expansion with a second Command Center, add a Tech Lab to the first Barracks and a Reactor to the second, throw down an extra Depot, and start researching Concussive Shells to improve your Marauders.

[Step 3] Build the first Barracks while keeping worker production steady, then start training a mix of Marines and Marauders from the rax, and throw down a couple of depots to stay ahead on supply.

[Step 4] Research combat shield while upgrading the Command Center to an Orbital Command, then add a tech lab to the Barracks and start producing a mix of marauders and marines, while keeping SCV production going.

[Step 5] Keep worker production steady at the Command Center while pumping out an early infantry mix from the Barracks — train a few Marines and a couple of Marauders. Toss down an extra depot to keep supply flowing and avoid any production blocks.

[Step 6] Continue steady SCV production while training a mix of Marines and Marauders from the Barracks to build up the bio army, and throw down a couple of Supply Depots to keep the supply cap from blocking further production.

[Step 7] Keep worker production steady while throwing down a couple of depots to prevent supply blocks, and reinforce your army by training several Marines and a pair of Marauders from your Barracks.
