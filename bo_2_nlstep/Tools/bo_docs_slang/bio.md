# Summary

This is a macro bio opening that goes for a fast expand with a Reaper scout, aiming for a strong Marine-Marauder-Medivac composition. The early rhythm is a standard 1-1-1 into a quick natural CC, then techs through Factory for a Siege Tank before dropping double Starports for Medivacs and a Viking. Gas is taken early and a third Refinery comes before the third CC, with upgrades prioritized: Concussive Shells, Stim, then Combat Shield. The late production direction is mass bio from multiple rax with Reactors and Tech Labs, supported by a steady flow of Medivacs, and a single Tank for positional play.

# Details

[Step 1] Start by throwing down a Supply Depot, then keep SCV production rolling while building your first Barracks and a Refinery. Continue training workers as you add a second Barracks, and finish this phase by producing a Reaper for early game scouting and harassment.

[Step 2] Upgrade the first Command Center to an Orbital Command for MULEs, while producing a couple of Marines and keeping SCV production going. Drop a second Command Center for the natural expansion, add another Depot, and attach a Reactor to the Barracks to double Marine output. Start a Factory to begin your tech transition, all while maintaining steady worker production.

[Step 3] Keep the macro humming by pumping Marines from the Barracks and SCVs from the Command Center, add a Tech Lab onto the Factory to unlock advanced tech, and throw down a cluster of Supply Depots to ensure you don't get supply blocked.

[Step 4] Morph the Command Center into an Orbital Command for extra scans and income, then start pumping Marines and a Siege Tank from the Barracks and Factory. Drop down a third Refinery to secure more gas, add a Tech Lab to the Barracks for future upgrades, and keep the SCV production humming with a couple of workers.

[Step 5] Keep pumping SCVs while adding two Starports and a second Barracks to transition into tech-heavy production. Kick off Concussive Shells research for upcoming Marauders and reinforce with a couple of Marines in the meantime.

[Step 6] Keep workers coming from the Command Center while adding depots to manage supply. Produce a Siege Tank from the Factory and reinforce with a few Marines and a Marauder from the Barracks, then start a tech lab on the rax to unlock stim or other upgrades.

[Step 7] Start researching Stimpack while pumping several marines and throwing down a pair of depots to keep supply up; continue steady SCV production from the Command Center.

[Step 8] Research Combat Shield while starting a steady stream of Marines from the rax and a Medivac from the starport, throw down two depots to keep supply open, and keep worker production humming from the CC.

[Step 9] Keep SCV production going while adding several depots to avoid supply blocks, then move out to take your natural expansion with a new Command Center. Follow up with another Barracks and train a couple of Marines to secure the area.

[Step 10] With the Starport churning out a Medivac and then a Viking for air control, reinforce the bio ball by training a few Marauders and several Marines, while adding a second Barracks and slapping a Reactor onto one of the Rax to ramp up production.

[Step 11] Add a rax with a reactor, train a Marauder and a few Marines, throw down a depot for supply, also add a reactor to the Starport, and take two refineries to ramp up gas income.

[Step 12] Morph the Command Center into an Orbital Command for extra scans and supply, then start pumping out a couple of marauders backed by a solid wave of marines to build up a strong bio force.

[Step 13] Continue pumping out Marauders and Marines from the Barracks, train a couple of Medivacs from the Starport for pickup and healing, and throw down a couple of depots to keep supply flowing as you prepare for a bio push.

[Step 14] Add a supply depot to keep up with production, then train a Marauder followed by half a dozen Marines for a solid bio push, and finish by dropping two more depots to stay ahead of the supply cap.

[Step 15] Pump out two Medivacs and a pair of Marauders alongside a handful of Marines, adding a depot to stay ahead on supply for the coming engagement.

[Step 16] Train a couple of Marauders and a wave of Marines, then throw down two Supply Depots to prevent supply block, and continue pumping out additional Marines to reinforce your bio army.

[Step 17] Ramp up bio production by churning out a wave of marines and a couple of marauders from the rax, while throwing down additional depots to keep the supply flowing and maintain a strong infantry ball.
