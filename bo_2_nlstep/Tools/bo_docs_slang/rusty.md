# Summary

Start with a standard 1-1-1 opener: depot, gas, rax, then OC the main while dropping a natural CC and a factory with tech lab for early tanks. After securing the natural, double gas both bases, add a second rax and a starport for medivacs, then tech lab one rax for combat shield while putting reactors on the rest. The core comp is marine-tank with medivac support and a viking for map control, aiming for a strong two-base mid-game timing push with constant worker and depot production to avoid supply blocks.

# Details

[Step 1] Kick off with a Supply Depot, then keep SCV production humming along. Throw down a Refinery and start your Barracks, then add another Depot to stay ahead on supply while continuing worker production.

[Step 2] Upgrade your first Command Center to an Orbital Command for improved mineral income and scan utility, then keep SCV production constant while training a couple of Marines for safety. Drop your second Command Center to secure the natural expansion, and add a Refinery to start gathering gas. Follow up with a Factory and its Tech Lab to open up mechanical tech, and throw down an extra Supply Depot to prevent supply blocks.

[Step 3] Morph the Command Center into an Orbital Command for faster income, keep pumping SCVs from both bases, and produce a couple Marines for defense while starting a Factory and the first Siege Tank to get your mechanical firepower online.

[Step 4] Drop down both refineries to secure gas income, continue pumping workers from the Command Center, train a Marine for a bit of defense, build a depot to keep supply open, start the first Siege Tank from the factory, then add the tech lab to the factory for further upgrades or tank production.

[Step 5] Keep up constant SCV production while adding a second Barracks and your first Starport for tech, then throw down several Supply Depots to keep your supply open for the upcoming unit wave.

[Step 6] Add a second and third Barracks, putting a Tech Lab on one of them for future Marauder tech, and start researching Combat Shield while training a few Marines. At the same time, the Factory rolls out two Siege Tanks for early pressure, while the Starport produces a Medivac and then immediately adds a Reactor to double up on Medivac production.

[Step 7] Produce two Siege Tanks from the Factory while adding several Supply Depots to avoid supply blocks, attach Reactors onto existing Barracks to enable double Marine production, and take your expansion with a fresh Command Center to secure a strong economy for the mid-game push.

[Step 8] Continue churning out Marines while producing a Medivac and a Viking for dropship and air support, then pump out a pair of Siege Tanks from the Factory before throwing down three additional Supply Depots to avoid getting supply blocked.

[Step 9] Pump out several marines from the rax, add a couple of tanks from the factory and a medivac for support, and throw down a couple of depots to prevent supply blocks.

[Step 10] Throw down a depot to free up supply, then pump out a wave of Marines from the Barracks—eight in total—and follow up with another depot to keep the production rolling.

[Step 11] Morph your Command Center into an Orbital Command for the mule, then start pumping out a couple of Medivacs, two Siege Tanks, and a handful of Marines to build up a strong mid-game army.

[Step 12] Produce a wave of Marines while adding a Viking for air support and a couple of Siege Tanks to fortify the ground army.

[Step 13] Pump out a wave of Marines from the Barracks to reinforce the army, creating a solid bio core for the early-mid game push.

[Step 14] Pump out a pair of Siege Tanks from the Factory while churning out a wave of Marines from your Barracks to reinforce your army for an upcoming push or to hold a potential attack.

[Step 15] Ramp up your bio force by training half a dozen Marines from the rax, while the factory produces a Siege Tank to provide siege support. To keep the supply flowing, throw down three additional depots.

[Step 16] Throw down several depots to keep supply flowing, produce a Siege Tank from the Factory for defensive support, and train a handful of Marines from the Barracks to reinforce the army.

[Step 17] Pump out a wave of Marines from the rax while adding a Siege Tank from the factory to bolster your defensive or pushing power.

[Step 18] Keep churning out Marines from your rax for a strong bio ball, while adding a Medivac to keep them alive and a Viking to gain air superiority or scout the map.

[Step 19] Reinforce the army by pumping out several Marines from the barracks, while the factory produces a Siege Tank and the starport adds a Medivac for support.

[Step 20] From the Starport, begin building a Medivac to provide healing and drop capability, while simultaneously training a large wave of Marines from the Barracks to bulk up your bio army.

[Step 21] Keep pumping out marines from the rax while adding a few supply depots to avoid getting supply blocked—drop a cluster of depots to keep the production flowing smoothly.

[Step 22] Use your Barracks to pump out a wave of Marines, queuing up over a dozen to build a solid bio force.
