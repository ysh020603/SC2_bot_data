# Summary

Open with a standard 1-1-1 into a fast third CC after the first hellion, using reapers for early map control and a raven for safety in TvT. Tech path goes rax -> factory -> starport, then add a cyclone and siege tank before dropping a second starport tech lab for vikings. From there, you slam down extra raxes with reactors, grab double e-bays for +1/+1 and stim, then mix in medivacs, tanks, and vikings while keeping constant worker production from both OCs. The build is a safe, macro-oriented opening that transitions into a heavy bio-tank composition with air support, aiming for a strong midgame push.

# Details

[Step 1] Start by keeping SCV production going while placing your first Supply Depot and then a Refinery to begin gas income. Follow up by dropping the first Barracks, adding a second Refinery, and continuing to pump out several SCVs to build a solid worker count for the early game.

[Step 2] Upgrade the starting Command Center to an Orbital Command and drop a Factory to begin hellion production. Keep workers flowing from both CCs, and train a pair of Reapers from the Barracks for early map control and harass. After the first Hellion, take a fast third expansion by dropping another Command Center, and continue reinforcing with a second Hellion, another Reaper, and extra depots to stay ahead on supply.

[Step 3] Add a Starport for air support and attach tech labs to both the Factory and Barracks for advanced upgrades. Start producing a Cyclone and a Siege Tank to build up a mech force, while beginning a Raven from the Starport for detection or harassment. At the same time, keep worker production steady, morph the existing Command Center into an Orbital Command to boost income, and drop a Refinery to secure extra gas.

[Step 4] Take the expansion by dropping the second Command Center, while continuously producing workers and adding depots to keep supply flowing. Train a Raven from the Starport for early detection or harass, and attach a Reactor to the Barracks to double Marine production.

[Step 5] Keep SCV production going while pumping a Siege Tank from the factory and a Viking from the starport. Add a couple of depots to free up supply, and start another Barracks to ramp up bio production.

[Step 6] Start adding extra Barracks to prepare for a heavy bio force, continue SCV production, push out a Siege Tank from the Factory, morph the existing Command Center into an Orbital Command, and produce a pair of Vikings from the Starport.

[Step 7] Keep SCV production going while adding a pair of Engineering Bays to kick off your upgrades. Secure your gas income with three Refineries and put a Reactor on your Barracks to double up on Marine training.

[Step 8] Kick off Stimpack and +1 infantry attack in the tech lab’d Barracks while you add a reactor to another rax for double marine production; keep SCVs coming from both OCs, throw down a depot to stay ahead on supply, and pop out a Viking for scouting or to answer early air threats.

[Step 9] Continue constant SCV production from both bases, add several supply depots to stay ahead on supply, start +1 infantry armor at the Engineering Bay, and produce a Viking for map vision or anti-air along with a Siege Tank to anchor your defense or support a push.

[Step 10] Keep SCV production humming while adding a few extra Barracks to ramp up bio production, then start producing your first Siege Tank from the Factory for early map control or defense.

[Step 11] Continue pumping out Marines from the Barracks while the Starport builds a Medivac for drop pressure or reinforcement, and throw down several Supply Depots to keep up with the growing army supply.

[Step 12] Drop down an Armory to enable mech upgrades, while pumping 5 Marines from the Barracks. Add a Reactor to one Barracks for faster production, throw down a depot to avoid getting supply blocked, and start producing a pair of Medivacs out of the Starport to provide healing and drop mobility.

[Step 13] Start producing a Siege Tank from the factory while throwing down a few extra depots to keep supply flowing. Attach a Reactor to the Barracks, then immediately begin reinforcing with a handful of Marines.

[Step 14] Research Combat Shield and start +1 vehicle weapons while reinforcing with several Marines and adding a couple of supply depots. Then produce a Liberator from the Starport for map control.

[Step 15] Research infantry +1 while adding several extra depots to keep up with supply, then produce a handful of marines and a single siege tank to reinforce the push.

[Step 16] Continue pumping out a wave of marines from the rax to build up your bio army, throw down a depot to avoid getting supply blocked, and start research on infantry armor to give your marines extra staying power in fights.

[Step 17] Transition into Factory tech by starting a Factory, then produce your first Siege Tank while also building a Viking from the Starport for air control. Keep the Barracks churning out a wave of Marines for ground support, and throw down a Supply Depot to maintain your production flow.

[Step 18] Continue pumping out a wave of marines from the rax to reinforce the army, and throw down a couple of depots to keep supply from capping.

[Step 19] Pump out a solid wave of Marines from the rax, add a Siege Tank from the factory, and produce a pair of Vikings from the starport for air control and map vision.

[Step 20] Keep churning out Marines from the rax while attaching a tech lab to the Factory for tech progression, and add a reactor to one of the barracks to double down on marine production.

[Step 21] Pump out a wave of Marines from the rax while the Starport produces two Vikings and the Factory adds a Siege Tank. Kick off mech +1 and keep reinforcing with additional Marines.
