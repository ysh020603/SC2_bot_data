# Summary

Open with a fast expand – drop your first rax and gas, then immediately plop down a second CC for an early natural and upgrade both to orbitals – then tech through Factory into Starport while slapping a Fusion Core for BCs. You’ll churn out Marines for early defense with a bunker, add a couple Siege Tanks from the Factory for map control, and then pivot hard into mass Battlecruisers, supported by a growing bio force of Marines (eventually with Combat Shield) and a second Starport with a tech lab. Keep constant SCV production, add extra raxes and depots to avoid supply blocks, and eventually take a third base as you queue up wave after wave of BCs backed by Marine-tank meat – pure macro into heavy air dominance.

# Details

[Step 1] Start by maintaining constant SCV production from your Command Center while throwing down a Supply Depot at the start. Once you have enough minerals, build your first Barracks and a Refinery, then continue training SCVs and add a second Supply Depot to keep up with the growing supply.

[Step 2] Morph your first Command Center into an Orbital Command for faster economy and scan potential, then drop a second CC for your natural expansion. Keep worker production humming from both bases while training a handful of Marines for early defense. Add your second Refinery to secure gas, then start the Factory to unlock tank or mech options, and throw down an extra Depot to avoid supply blocks.

[Step 3] As your first Marines pop out, throw down a Bunker at the front for early defense while starting up the Starport for your tech transition. Morph the natural Command Center into an Orbital Command to boost economy, and keep pumping SCVs. Add the second Barracks to ramp up Marine production, drop a Refinery for gas income, and slap a Tech Lab on the Factory to unlock Siege Tank or Cyclone tech.

[Step 4] Drop the Fusion Core to enable Battlecruiser tech, and add a Tech Lab to the Starport along with an extra Refinery to support the gas cost. Keep macroing with a few SCVs and Marines off one Barracks, and round out the production with a Siege Tank from the Factory for map control and defense.

[Step 5] Keep pumping SCVs from the Command Center while training a couple of Marines from the Barracks, and throw down a depot to avoid getting supply blocked as you continue building your economy and early army.

[Step 6] Start building a Battlecruiser from the Starport while keeping up constant Marine and worker production from your Rax and Orbital. To support the growing army, throw down a few extra depots to avoid getting supply blocked.

[Step 7] Keep SCV production going from the Command Center, add a few depots to manage supply, and train several Marines from the Barracks while the Factory pumps out two Siege Tanks to bolster your defensive line.

[Step 8] Add another Barracks with a tech lab for tech upgrades, while starting a Battlecruiser from your existing Starport and throwing down two depots to avoid supply blocks. Put a reactor on your other Barracks for faster Marine production, then build a second Starport and add a tech lab to it. Round out the phase by training a Siege Tank from the Factory and a handful of Marines for defensive support.

[Step 9] Research Combat Shield, then throw down two additional Barracks. Start the first Battlecruiser from the Starport, while training several Marines from the rax for defense. Follow up with a second BC, reinforce with more Marines, and add a couple of Supply Depots to avoid supply block.

[Step 10] First, take another expansion by dropping a Command Center, then produce a Siege Tank from the Factory, follow up by pumping out a wave of Marines from your Barracks, and finally queue up a Battlecruiser from the Starport for heavy air support.

[Step 11] From your Barracks, keep cranking out waves of marines to strengthen your bio force, and add a couple of depots to prevent supply blocks as you ramp up production.

[Step 12] Start pumping out a Battlecruiser from the starport while the rax and factory keep producing—churn out a handful of Marines and a Siege Tank to reinforce the army, and throw down another depot to keep supply flowing.

[Step 13] Morph the CC into an orbital, then start building a BC from the Starport while pumping out a wave of Marines from the Barracks and adding a Siege Tank from the Factory to support the push.

[Step 14] Pump out a wave of Marines from the rax, then kick off your first Battlecruiser from the Starport. Keep the Marine production rolling and throw down a depot to ensure you don't get supply blocked as you head into the late game.

[Step 15] Mass up 6 Marines for early pressure while simultaneously adding three depots to keep the supply headroom for continuous marine production.
