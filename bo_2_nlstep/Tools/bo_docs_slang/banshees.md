# Summary

This build goes for a fast expand—dropping a second CC after the first OC—then opens with a Rax into Factory into Starport, teching up with Tech Labs on both the Factory and Starport to rush out Siege Tanks and Banshees for early harassment and defense. The bot keeps worker production humming, adds a Bunker for safety, and gradually transitions into a Marine-Tank-Banshee composition, pumping Marines from a Reactor Rax while producing Tanks and a steady stream of Banshees (eventually grabbing cloak via a Starport Tech Lab). After researching Combat Shield, it adds a second Starport and a third Rax, then takes a third CC to support constant marine/tank/banshee reinforcement, aiming for a mid-game pressure/macro mix rather than an all-in timing.

# Details

[Step 1] Start by throwing down a Supply Depot to avoid early supply blocks, then keep SCV production going from the Command Center. Begin setting up your first Barracks and Refinery, making sure to queue up several more SCVs in the process. Add a second depot before you hit the supply cap so you can keep building workers and prepare for your first units.

[Step 2] Morph the main Command Center into an Orbital Command while dropping a second CC for an expansion, and keep pumping workers and marines. Add a Refinery and start your Factory for early mech tech, and throw down an extra depot to avoid supply blocks.

[Step 3] Kick off this phase by starting a Starport and a Bunker for defense, then upgrade your existing Command Center to an Orbital Command for extra income. Keep worker production going and train a single Marine. Add another Barracks, and follow up by attaching Tech Labs to both the Factory and the Starport to unlock your tech options.

[Step 4] Keep SCV production humming from the Command Center while the rax cranks out a couple of Marines for map presence. From the factory, roll out a Siege Tank for defensive positioning or a push, and from the starport, produce a Banshee for harassment or scouting. Don't forget to add a depot to keep supply open.

[Step 5] Keep worker production going while training several Marines, throw down a couple of depots to avoid supply blocks, then produce a Siege Tank from the Factory, and continue reinforcing with Marines as you add more depots and workers.

[Step 6] Start training a Banshee from the first Starport while adding a second Barracks. Keep pumping workers and throw a Tech Lab on the original Barracks and a Reactor on the new one for double Marine production. Then, build a second Starport and an extra Refinery to support the gas-heavy aggression, all while maintaining constant SCV production.

[Step 7] Keep SCV production constant while adding depots to maintain supply. Produce a Siege Tank from the Factory for ground control, then follow up with a Banshee from the Starport. Train a couple of Marines for map presence and additional defense. Drop a third Refinery to support your high-gas army, and cap off this phase by attaching a Tech Lab to the Starport to unlock cloak or further air upgrades.

[Step 8] Research Combat Shield to upgrade your bio durability, crank out a batch of Marines from the rax, and produce a Banshee from the Starport for harassment. Add a couple of depots to manage supply, and keep training a few more Marines to bolster your army.

[Step 9] Keep pumping Marines out of the rax while your factory produces a Siege Tank and your starport adds a Banshee for harassment. Throw down two more depots to avoid supply blocks, then continue reinforcing the army with four additional Marines to support your push.

[Step 10] Add several depots to avoid getting supply blocked, train a few Marines to bolster the army, and produce a Banshee and a Siege Tank to apply pressure or shore up defense.

[Step 11] Keep the rax churning out marines while the starport produces a Banshee for harassment. Drop a couple of depots to keep supply flowing, and reinforce with a steady stream of marines to support the Banshee.

[Step 12] Start producing a Siege Tank from the factory and a pair of Banshees from the starport to set up a harassing or defensive force. Keep your supply flowing by adding depots, and train a handful of Marines to reinforce your army.

[Step 13] Start by throwing down a depot and then pump a batch of 4 Marines. Produce a Siege Tank from the Factory, followed by another Marine, then drop a couple more depots and start building a Banshee out of the Starport. Keep reinforcing with another 4 Marines.

[Step 14] Add a Barracks and start churning out Marines while throwing down several depots to avoid supply blocks; then produce a Banshee from the Starport and a Siege Tank from the Factory, reinforcing with a few more Marines before training a second Banshee for harassment or defense.

[Step 15] Drop two more raxes to reinforce your bio production, and take a third expansion to support the growing army. Keep pumping out a few Marines and Siege Tanks from the factory while adding several Banshees from the starport. Don't forget to throw down depots to keep supply flowing.
