# Summary

Open with a fast OC and a single rax, then rush a Factory with a Tech Lab while throwing down two Bunkers at the nat—this is a pure one‑base turtle defense aimed at sieging up early. You never take a third CC or expand; instead you rely on constant Marine production from two rax and a steady stream of Siege Tanks from the Factory to lock down your position. No Starport or late tech, just bio‑tank on one base with heavy supply‑depot discipline to hold until you can push out with a tank‑heavy ball of marines and siege mode.

# Details

[Step 1] Start the game by keeping constant SCV production from the Command Center, throw down two supply depots to avoid getting supply blocked, then build the first Barracks and Refinery to kick off your tech path, and finally train an initial Marine for scouting or early defense.

[Step 2] Morph the Command Center into an Orbital Command to saturate the mineral line faster, then start producing the first Marine from the Barracks while dropping the Factory for tech progression. Keep the SCV train going, add another Depot to keep supply flowing, and throw down a pair of Bunkers with a Refinery to secure the natural expansion and prepare for gas-heavy unit composition. Follow up with a couple more Marines to reinforce the early defense.

[Step 3] Drop the first Factory with a Tech Lab, start Siege Tank production, and add a second Barracks while maintaining SCV and Marine output from the Command Center and initial Rax — a standard transition into mech play with a Tank and a stronger bio backbone.

[Step 4] Start building the first Barracks, then pump out 2 Siege Tanks and several Marines while adding depots to keep up with production.

[Step 5] Keep churning out Marines from the rax while the Factory pushes out a pair of Siege Tanks to solidify your position, and drop a Depot to ensure you don't get supply blocked.

[Step 6] Keep churning out Marines in waves while throwing down depots to keep supply flowing, and produce a Siege Tank to add some serious punch to the ground force.

[Step 7] Continue pumping out Marines from the Barracks while cycling out three Siege Tanks from the Factory, building up a solid bio/tank core for the mid-game push.

[Step 8] Start by pumping out three Marines from the rax, then pull out your first Siege Tank from the Factory, followed by four more Marines—while throwing down a Depot to stay ahead on supply—and finish the phase with a second Siege Tank.

[Step 9] Produce a wave of Marines from the Barracks alongside a couple of Siege Tanks from the Factory, reinforcing the army with a solid mix of infantry and siege support.

[Step 10] Add a pair of depots to keep supply flowing while you churn out a wave of Marines and a couple of Siege Tanks from the Factory, building a solid bio-mech composition for map control.
