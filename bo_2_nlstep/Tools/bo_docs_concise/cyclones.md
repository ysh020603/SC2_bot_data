# Summary

Rusty Locks opens with a super fast expand and a quick third CC, rushing out a factory for early cyclone and hellion production with blue flame and lock-on. In the mid game, he throws down a bunch of factories with a mix of reactors and tech labs, cranks out hellions and cyclones for constant pressure, and picks up +1 vehicle weapons while slamming down a PF at the nat. Late game, he slams down multiple armories for mass upgrades and drowns the opponent in a relentless tide of upgraded hellions and cyclones from three bases, aiming to max out and roll over the enemy.
