# Summary

The opener goes double Rax into two Refineries, a Reaper for scouting, and a fast second CC for a quick expand, with both Rax getting a Tech Lab and Reactor to churn out Marauders and start Concussive Shells. In the mid game, the build scales to three Rax with two Reactors for constant double Marine production, grabs a third base while researching Combat Shield, and relies on a heavy bio ball of Marines and Marauders for sustained pressure. Late game sees nothing but mass Marine spam from Reactored Rax, backed by a strong economy on three bases, using endless waves of cheap bio to overwhelm the opponent through sheer numbers.

# Details

[Step 1] Start by throwing down a Supply Depot, then keep your SCV production going. Drop your first Barracks and Refinery, train a couple more workers, and then quickly add a second Barracks and a second Refinery to establish a strong early production setup.

[Step 2] Morph the first Command Center into an Orbital Command and start producing a Reaper along with a couple of Marines for early map presence and defense. Keep SCV production flowing and drop a second Command Center to secure an expansion. Add a Tech Lab and a Reactor onto the Barracks, then begin researching Concussive Shells to strengthen your bio force.

[Step 3] Keep SCV production going from the Command Center while putting the first Barracks to work, training a Marauder and a Marine to build a solid bio core. Add a Supply Depot to stay ahead of the supply cap, then squeeze in another SCV and a second Marauder before throwing down an additional depot to keep the production rolling.

[Step 4] Morph the Command Center into an Orbital Command for extra scans and mules, start researching Combat Shield for your bio, and add a tech lab to the first Barracks. Keep worker production steady while mixing in a few Marauders and Marines to build a solid core for the mid-game.

[Step 5] Continue pumping out several SCVs from the Command Center while the Barracks trains a few Marines and a couple of Marauders to build up the bio force, and throw down an extra depot to keep the supply flowing smoothly.

[Step 6] Continue pumping SCVs from the Command Center while reinforcing with a mix of Marines and Marauders from the Barracks, and throw down two additional Supply Depots to support the growing army.

[Step 7] Keep SCV production steady while training a mix of Marines and Marauders from the Barracks to build up the bio army. Add a couple of Supply Depots to avoid supply blocks, and reinforce with more bio units at the same time to prepare for the next engagement.

[Step 8] Drop additional depots to keep supply headroom, keep churning out workers, and build a few Marauders supported by a handful of Marines for a solid bio core.

[Step 9] Expand to the third while adding a second and third Barracks, and continue aggressive Marauder production with a few Marines for support, maintaining constant SCV output and throwing down depots to keep supply open.

[Step 10] Add a third Barracks and throw down an extra Depot to support the growing bio army, then train a couple of Marauders along with several Marines to maintain unit count. Once the rax are up, attach Reactors to two of them for faster double Marine production.

[Step 11] Drop the first two refineries to kickstart gas income, then begin training a Marauder while pumping out waves of Marines. Morph the Command Center into an Orbital Command for extra supply and mule drops, keeping the infantry count up to maintain map presence.

[Step 12] Keep cranking out Marines for map presence while adding depots to avoid supply blocks, then slap a reactor on the rax to double up your marine production.

[Step 13] Add a depot to keep your supply ready, then pump out a wave of Marines from the rax to bolster your early army presence.

[Step 14] Throw down a couple of depots to avoid supply blocks while pumping out eight Marines from the rax to reinforce your army.

[Step 15] Start by training a single Marine, then throw down two depots to keep supply ahead. After that, pump out a wave of Marines—about half a dozen—and finally add another depot to ensure you don't get supply blocked while continuing to build up your army.

[Step 16] Throw down two supply depots to keep the supply flowing, then start pumping out a wave of marines from the barracks to build up the army.

[Step 17] Start by producing a Marauder from the Barracks, then add a Supply Depot followed by a Marine, and then quickly throw down a few more depots while training a wave of Marines to maintain a strong bio army.

[Step 18] Now it's time to start pumping out a sizeable marine force while ensuring you don't get supply blocked—throw down two additional depots and queue up a steady stream of marines from your barracks to build up a strong early army.

[Step 19] Throw down two depots to secure supply, then train eight Marines from the Barracks to reinforce.

[Step 20] Throw down a couple of depots to free up supply, and pump out a solid wave of marines from the rax to bolster the bio ball.

[Step 21] Churn out a massive wave of Marines from your barracks, then add two reactors to ramp up production for the next wave.

[Step 22] This is a macro-oriented bio spam build that aims to drown the opponent in relentless waves of cheap infantry, using efficient production and a fast third base to sustain constant pressure.
