# Summary

Open with a standard 1-rax expand into Orbital, then grab gas and a Factory into a Starport while adding a Bunker for safety, then rush a Fusion Core for early BC tech. Ramp up with double-Barracks production, one Reactor and one Tech Lab, while cranking out Siege Tanks and starting your first Battlecruiser, then drop a third CC and start churning Marines for a marine–tank–BC midgame that pressures with the BC while you build up a bio ball. Transition into mass Battlecruisers from two Starports with Tech Labs, supported by a marine–tank ground army on three bases, aiming to overwhelm with BC Yamato shots and sustained bio reinforcement.

# Details

[Step 1] Start by building a Supply Depot and keep your SCVs training non-stop from the Command Center. Add the first Barracks and a Refinery to secure early gas, then throw down another Depot to avoid supply blocks while continuing worker production.

[Step 2] Upgrade your main Command Center to an Orbital for scans and mules, then drop a second Command Center for your expansion. Keep pumping workers while training a few Marines, and take your first gas with a Refinery. Follow up by throwing down a Factory to kick off your tech path, and add a Supply Depot to keep your supply in check.

[Step 3] With the Factory up, start the first Starport for medivac or banshee options, throw down a Bunker for defense, and morph the main Command Center into an Orbital Command for extra income from MULEs. Keep SCV production going while adding a second Barracks for more bio reinforcements, grab another Refinery to secure gas income, and attach a Tech Lab to the Factory so you can research Siege Mode or Drilling Claws. Train a few Marines to fill out the initial army and hold the Bunker.

[Step 4] Build the Fusion Core to unlock Battlecruisers, then add a Tech Lab on the Starport and another Refinery. Keep SCV production going, reinforce with a couple of Marines, and round things off with a Siege Tank for defensive strength.

[Step 5] Keep the macro cycle rolling by training several SCVs from the Command Center while pumping out a few Marines from the Barracks, and throw down an additional Supply Depot to keep your supply from blocking.

[Step 6] Start cranking out a Battlecruiser from the Starport while keeping your rax and CC humming — train a handful of Marines for defense and a few SCVs to keep the economy chugging, and don't forget to throw down three supply depots to ensure you don't get supply blocked.

[Step 7] Add a couple of depots to free up supply, then start cranking out a few workers while the Factory pumps out the first two Siege Tanks. Keep the Barracks active with a handful of Marines for defense, and don't forget to throw down more depots as you go to avoid getting supply capped.

[Step 8] Add an extra Barracks with a Tech Lab while placing a Reactor on your other rax to start churning out Marines. Begin your first Battlecruiser from the Starport, throw down two more depots to avoid supply blocks, and build a second Starport with a Tech Lab to double up on BC production. Meanwhile, reinforce with a Siege Tank from the Factory and a few Marines for early defense while the BC gets online.

[Step 9] Research Combat Shield while adding a couple more Barracks to ramp up bio production. Start your first Battlecruiser, then train a wave of Marines, followed by the second BC and a few extra Marines. Drop a couple of Supply Depots to keep the build flowing smoothly.

[Step 10] Drop a new Command Center to secure your expansion, follow up with a Siege Tank for defense, pump out a wave of Marines, and start building a Battlecruiser for a powerful late-game transition.

[Step 11] Start churning out a wave of Marines from the Barracks to build up a strong early bio force, then place a couple of depots to keep supply flowing, and finish this phase by reinforcing with a few more Marines to maintain pressure.

[Step 12] Pop the first Battlecruiser from the Starport while pumping out a wave of Marines from the Barracks and a Siege Tank from the Factory. Don't forget to throw down an extra depot to keep your production flowing smoothly.

[Step 13] Morph the Command Center into an Orbital Command, then start a Battlecruiser from the Starport and a Siege Tank from the Factory, while reinforcing with a wave of Marines from the Barracks to support your advancing army.

[Step 14] Queue up a Battlecruiser from the Starport while churning out a wave of Marines from the Barracks, and throw down an extra depot to keep your supply headroom open.

[Step 15] Keep churning out Marines from the rax to build up a solid bio force, and throw down a couple of depots to ensure you don't get supply blocked as the army grows.

[Step 16] A fast BC opening that transitions into a marine-tank midgame, using the early Battlecruiser to harass and buy time for a macro lead into mass BCs. Use this gameplan as your strategic baseline -- adapt your decisions based on what you scout and how the game unfolds.
