# Summary

Open with a standard rax-first expand, grab gas, throw down a factory with a tech lab for siege mode, double up on factories, and morph both CCs to orbitals for MULEs and scans. Mid game you drop a massive wave of raxes—up to 10—with reactors and a tech lab for combat shield, then pump a relentless bio-tank mix of marines and tanks to siege up and roll over the opponent. Late game you take a third base for more macro, keep cranking marines and tanks off that production, and aim for a maxed-out two-base timing push that never lets up.

# Details

[Step 1] Start by constantly producing SCVs from the Command Center, then throw down a supply depot followed by the first Barracks and a Refinery to get the early economy and tech structure going.

[Step 2] Expand by dropping a second Command Center at the natural while adding a depot to keep supply ahead. Keep SCV production steady, train a couple of Marines for map presence, then throw down a Refinery and start the Factory to transition into mech tech.

[Step 3] Keep pumping workers and throw down multiple depots to secure supply, add a tech lab on the existing factory, then start a second factory to ramp up mech production.

[Step 4] Start by queuing a handful of workers while producing the first Siege Tank from the factory for early defensive pressure, then keep pumping SCVs, add a tech lab to the factory for Siege Mode access, and throw down an extra supply depot to stay ahead of supply.

[Step 5] Start gas mining by constructing a Refinery while pumping out workers and begin producing a Siege Tank from the Factory for defensive strength. To support the expanding army and economy, add several depots in quick succession to prevent supply blocks.

[Step 6] Build the second Barracks with a Tech Lab attached and begin Combat Shield research. Meanwhile, start Siege Tank production from the Factory and take an additional Refinery to meet the growing gas demand. Don't forget to keep SCV production running non-stop.

[Step 7] Morph both Command Centers into Orbitals for extra vision and MULEs, then start pumping out Siege Tanks and a few Marines to bolster the army, adding a pair of Supply Depots to keep the production flowing.

[Step 8] Throw down a massive wave of raxes — 8 total — along with a couple of depots to start flooding the map with a bio army.

[Step 9] Add two more Barracks, equip one with a Reactor, then crank out a mix of Marines and Siege Tanks—train a few Marines from the rax while the Factory pumps out three Siege Tanks in a row, then finish by adding two more Reactors to the Barracks for sustained bio-mech production.

[Step 10] Drop your first Factory and start cranking out Siege Tanks while keeping your Barracks producing a steady stream of Marines to support the early mech push.

[Step 11] Continue producing a wave of Marines from the Barracks while starting a Tech Lab on the Factory for tech access, and add a few Supply Depots to keep up with the growing army count.

[Step 12] Drop a massive wave of supply depots to ensure you have enough supply to support your growing army and avoid getting blocked as you continue to produce.

[Step 13] Keep depots ahead of your supply while reinforcing with 3 Siege Tanks and 6 Marines to build a solid bio-mech force.

[Step 14] Crank out a wave of marines from your rax to build up the bio count, and produce a Siege Tank from the factory to add siege support, setting up a strong mid-game push or defensive posture.

[Step 15] Produce 2 Siege Tanks from the factory and a wave of Marines from the barracks to reinforce your army.

[Step 16] Pump out a wave of Marines from the rax while adding the first Siege Tank for firepower, setting up a solid bio-tank composition.

[Step 17] Pump out a wave of Marines from the rax to build up your army, reinforcing your early bio forces for the mid-game push.

[Step 18] Drop an expansion and upgrade it to an Orbital Command for extra macro, then crank out a wave of Marines from your rax to build up a solid early force.

[Step 19] A relentless two-base bio-tank timing that snowballs through rapid production and siege pushes, overwhelming opponents with sustained pressure. Use this gameplan as your strategic baseline -- adapt your decisions based on what you scout and how the game unfolds.
