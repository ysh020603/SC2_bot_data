# Summary

Open with a Reaper into fast expand, dropping a second Rax and a Factory with tech lab to start Siege Tank production while getting a Starport for Medivac tech. Mid-game, go up to four Rax with reactors and tech labs, pump Marines and Marauders with Stim and Combat Shield, and use Medivacs to push with the bio-tank army while snatching a quick third. Late-game, keep adding Rax and OCs to sustain a constant flood of bio backed by Siege Tanks and Medivacs, aiming to max out and roll over the opponent with relentless trades.

# Details

[Step 1] Begin by placing a Supply Depot and keeping constant SCV production from the Command Center. Follow up with your first Barracks and a Refinery to start collecting gas. Add a second Barracks while continuing worker production, then train a Reaper for early map control and harassment.

[Step 2] Morph the first Command Center into an Orbital Command for extra scans and supply, then train a couple of Marines from the Barracks while you add a depot and throw down a second Command Center for the natural expansion. With the Barracks, start a Reactor to double marine production, and get a Factory ready for the next tech phase, making sure to keep worker production steady.

[Step 3] Keep SCV production steady while cranking out a handful of Marines from the Barracks for map presence and defense. Add a tech lab to the Factory to unlock advanced tech, and throw down four Supply Depots to ensure you don't get supply blocked as you ramp up production.

[Step 4] Make an Orbital Command, throw down a Refinery, and start producing your first Siege Tank while adding a Tech Lab to the Barracks—keep pumping SCVs from the CC and churning out several Marines.

[Step 5] Start the first Starport, add a Barracks, and then throw down a second Starport while keeping worker production steady. Begin researching Concussive Shells to set up Marauder tech, and train a few Marines for early defense.

[Step 6] Keep SCV production constant to maintain economy, add depots as needed, and start producing a Siege Tank from the Factory while reinforcing the bio army with Marines and a Marauder. As units come out, attach a Tech Lab to the Barracks to enable further Marauder production.

[Step 7] Keep SCV production rolling while starting Stimpack research at the Barracks. Add a couple of Supply Depots to keep the supply headroom up, and train a few Marines to beef up the army.

[Step 8] Research Combat Shield while producing a few Marines, throw down a couple of depots to avoid supply blocks, keep SCVs coming from the command center, and start a Medivac for healing and drop mobility.

[Step 9] Keep worker production going and produce a couple Marines for defense while throwing down several Supply Depots to handle the incoming supply. Begin taking an expansion with a new Command Center, and add another Barracks to reinforce your bio production for the mid-game.

[Step 10] Continue reinforcing with a Medivac, several Marauders, and Marines while adding a second Barracks for extra production. Build a Viking for air support and attach a Reactor to one of the Barracks to double Marine output.

[Step 11] Start adding a second Barracks and produce a Marauder, then throw down a Depot for supply. Attach a Reactor to that Barracks to quickly pump out three Marines, and follow up by building a Reactor on your Starport while taking two Refineries to secure gas for your tech transition.

[Step 12] Morph the Command Center into an Orbital Command for extra scans and supply, then pump out a couple of Marauders and a wave of Marines to reinforce your bio army.

[Step 13] Keep pumping marauders and marines from all barracks while producing the first pair of medivacs from the Starport to start building the bio-medivac army. Add two depots to keep supply from blocking, ensuring you have enough pop for the next wave of units.

[Step 14] Throw down a depot to keep supply ahead, then start pumping out a mixed bio force—train a Marauder backed by a wave of Marines for a strong push—and add a couple more depots to support the growing army.

[Step 15] Keep producing from both the Starport and Barracks to reinforce your bio army, adding a couple of Medivacs for mobility and a couple of Marauders backed by a wave of Marines, while also throwing down a Depot to keep your supply from capping.

[Step 16] Pump out 2 Marauders and a handful of Marines to reinforce your bio army, while throwing down a couple of depots to keep supply flowing.

[Step 17] Start by training a couple of marines and two marauders from your rax, then throw down a few depots to keep supply open. Follow up by pumping out a wave of marines, add another pair of depots, and finish the phase by reinforcing with two more marauders to strengthen your bio army.

[Step 18] This is a relentless bio-tank flood that leverages early Reaper harassment into a constant stream of Marines, Marauders, and Siege Tanks, trading efficiently and denying your opponent breathing room through sheer production volume. Use this gameplan as your strategic baseline -- adapt your decisions based on what you scout and how the game unfolds.
