# Summary

This is a macro-oriented Terran build that opens with a fast expansion after a standard Barracks and Refinery, morphing the second Command Center into an Orbital Command before prioritizing tech progression through Factory and Starport. The early game rhythm focuses on steady SCV production from two bases, early Marine defense, and a Bunker, while attaching Tech Labs to both the Factory and Starport to unlock Siege Tanks and Banshees for early harassment and map control. The tech path follows a strict Barracks → Factory → Starport order, later adding a second Starport and multiple Refineries to support gas-heavy production. The main unit composition combines mass Marines from up to six Barracks with a mech backbone of Siege Tanks and Banshees for sustained pressure and harassment. Final production direction emphasizes continuous bio–mech reinforcement with an expanding economy, adding more Barracks, a fourth base, and ongoing Banshee and Tank output to support a decisive mid-game push.

# Details

[Step 1] Start by building a Supply Depot, then produce a couple of SCVs before constructing your first Barracks and a Refinery. Continue training several more SCVs and finish the phase by adding a second Supply Depot to support further production.

[Step 2] Morph the first Command Center into an Orbital Command while dropping a second Command Center for a fast expansion. Add a second Refinery and begin constructing a Factory for tech progression. Keep SCV production steady from both bases and train several Marines from the Barracks for early defense. Build another Supply Depot to prevent supply block as the economy grows.

[Step 3] Expand to a second base and morph it into an Orbital Command while starting a Starport and a Bunker for defense. Keep producing SCVs, train a Marine, and add a second Barracks to ramp up production. Then attach Tech Labs to both the Factory and the Starport to unlock advanced tech options.

[Step 4] Pump out SCVs from both Orbital Commands while training a couple of Marines for defense. Build a Siege Tank from the Factory and a Banshee from the Starport to apply early pressure, and add another Supply Depot to prevent supply block.

[Step 5] Keep your SCV production going from both Orbital Commands while training a few Marines from the Barracks. Build a pair of Supply Depots to secure your supply headroom, then pump out additional workers and a Siege Tank from the Factory. Reinforce with more Marines, drop another pair of Supply Depots, and continue the steady stream of SCVs to keep your economy strong.

[Step 6] Build a third Barracks and begin production of another Banshee from the existing Starport while training several SCVs. Add a Tech Lab and a Reactor to the Barracks for simultaneous research and mass marine production, then set down a second Starport and a third Refinery to ramp up gas-heavy unit output.

[Step 7] Reinforce the army with a Siege Tank and a Banshee to strengthen the mech push, while keeping the Barracks and Command Centers busy with a few Marines and SCVs. Secure the economy with a fourth Refinery and shore up supply with several Depots, then start attaching a Tech Lab to the Starport for future upgrades.

[Step 8] Research Combat Shield, keep training Marines from all three Barracks to reinforce the army, add two more Supply Depots to support the growing supply, and produce an additional Banshee from a Starport for harass potential.

[Step 9] Produce a steady stream of Marines from the three Barracks while reinforcing your mech presence with another Siege Tank from the Factory and a Banshee from the Starport to maintain harassment pressure. To avoid supply blocks, place a pair of additional Supply Depots.

[Step 10] Keep the production lines humming: train a few Marines from the Barracks, start one Banshee at the Starport, and add a Siege Tank from the Factory to your mech force. To support this heavy bio–mech–air output, drop several Supply Depots across your bases to prevent supply blocks and maintain momentum for the upcoming push.

[Step 11] Keep the Barracks pumping out Marines for the main army, produce another Banshee for continued harassment, and drop a couple of Supply Depots to prevent supply block as the forces grow.

[Step 12] Reinforce the army by producing a Siege Tank from the Factory and two Banshees from the Starports, while the Barracks train a handful of Marines. Build additional Supply Depots to keep supply from capping.

[Step 13] Reinforce the army with a wave of Marines, a Siege Tank, and a Banshee, while adding several Supply Depots to support the growing supply count.

[Step 14] Add a fourth Barracks to boost Marine production, train several Marines and a Siege Tank while producing a couple of Banshees from the Starports, and build additional Supply Depots to support the growing army, reinforcing your bio-mech forces for an upcoming push.

[Step 15] Add two more Barracks to ramp up Marine production, and take another expansion with a new Command Center. Continue reinforcing your army with Marines, Siege Tanks from the Factory, and Banshees from the Starports, while making sure to build extra Supply Depots to support the growing force.
