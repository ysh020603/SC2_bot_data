# Summary

This build order is a macro-oriented, defensive Terran strategy that prioritizes a fast expansion with an early Command Center and Orbital Command, followed by a tech path of Barracks into Factory with Tech Lab, and then a Starport with Reactor. The core army composition relies heavily on Marines for mass infantry and Siege Tanks for positional defense, supported by Medivacs for mobility and a few Vikings for anti-air. Production ramps up through multiple Barracks with Reactors and continuous tank production, aiming for a strong mid-game bio-mech timing push while maintaining a solid defensive line.

# Details

[Step 1] Produce 6 SCVs while constructing 2 Supply Depots, a Refinery, and a Barracks to secure your supply cap and start gas income for early tech.

[Step 2] Take an expansion with a new Command Center and morph it into an Orbital Command, build a Refinery for gas, and construct a Factory with a Tech Lab for advanced production, while training 6 SCVs and 2 Marines for defense and supply management behind a Supply Depot.

[Step 3] Transition into Factory tech by building a Factory, train 2 Siege Tanks and 2 Marines for defense, produce 4 SCVs to maintain economy, and morph the Command Center into an Orbital Command.

[Step 4] Build a Supply Depot to free up supply, then construct 2 Refineries for gas income and attach a Tech Lab to the Factory. While producing 8 SCVs to accelerate your economy, train 1 Marine for light defense and start your first Siege Tank to establish a strong positional threat.

[Step 5] Build the first and second Barracks alongside a Starport, train 8 SCVs, and erect 4 Supply Depots to support the expanding infrastructure and unit production.

[Step 6] Build the first and second Barracks, add a Tech Lab to one and a Reactor to the Starport, then produce 2 Siege Tanks, 1 Medivac, and 3 Marines while researching ShieldWall to enhance your tank survivability.

[Step 7] Construct 4 Supply Depots for increased supply capacity, then attach Reactors to 3 Barracks to enable double marine production. Build a new Command Center to secure a third expansion, and train 2 Siege Tanks for defensive positioning and mid-game army reinforcement.

[Step 8] Build three Supply Depots to maintain production capacity, then train 3 Marines, 1 Medivac, 1 Viking, and 2 Siege Tanks to create a balanced mid-game force capable of both defense and pushing.

[Step 9] Build two Supply Depots to support your growing army, then train 5 Marines, 2 Siege Tanks, and 1 Medivac to set up a solid defensive line with tank siege potential and medivac mobility.

[Step 10] Build 2 Supply Depots to increase your supply cap, and produce 8 Marines to establish a strong defensive army for early map control.

[Step 11] Morph the Command Center into an Orbital Command while training 5 Marines, 2 Siege Tanks, and 2 Medivacs to strengthen your army composition for an upcoming mid-game engagement.

[Step 12] Train 7 Marines, 1 Viking, and 2 Siege Tanks to create a solid mid-game army with ground firepower, anti-air coverage, and siege support for pushes or defense.

[Step 13] Train 10 Marines to reinforce your army for a mid-game timing push or to hold off enemy aggression.

[Step 14] Train 2 Siege Tanks and 8 Marines to fortify your defensive line and prepare a strong bio–mech force for mid-game engagements.

[Step 15] Construct 3 additional Supply Depots to support your growing army, produce 6 Marines for a solid bio core, and deploy 1 Siege Tank to provide defensive firepower and zone control.

[Step 16] Construct 6 Supply Depots to support your expanding army, train a Siege Tank for siege defense or push, and produce 3 Marines to bolster your core infantry.

[Step 17] Train 9 Marines and 1 Siege Tank to reinforce your army, providing a strong defensive backbone with siege capability.

[Step 18] Train 8 Marines, 1 Medivac, and 1 Viking Fighter to build a versatile mid-game force capable of ground support and air denial.

[Step 19] Produce 8 Marines, 1 Siege Tank, and 1 Medivac to strengthen your mid-game army composition for map control and push readiness.

[Step 20] Produce 1 Medivac and 9 Marines to reinforce the main army.

[Step 21] Build 5 Supply Depots to raise your supply cap, and train 5 Marines to reinforce your army.

[Step 22] Produce 14 Marines to reinforce your army.
