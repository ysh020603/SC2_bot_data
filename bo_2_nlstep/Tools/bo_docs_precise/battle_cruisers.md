# Summary

This build order executes a defensive macro opening, fast expanding with an early Orbital Command and double Refinery before transitioning through a Factory into a Starport, then rushing to a Fusion Core to unlock Battlecruisers as the primary late-game weapon. The early game focuses on Marine production and a Siege Tank for positioning, while a Bunker reinforces the natural. Tech labs are added to the Factory and Starport, and additional Barracks with a Tech Lab support Marine upgrades like Shield Wall. The composition revolves around Battlecruisers backed by Siege Tanks and Marines for ground control, with continuous SCV production, Supply Depots, and a third Command Center to sustain a heavy air-based macro army.

# Details

[Step 1] Train 6 SCVs from your Command Center while constructing 2 Supply Depots, a Barracks, and a Refinery to secure an early economy and tech structure.

[Step 2] Train 6 SCVs and 4 Marines while constructing a Supply Depot, Refinery, and Factory; then build a Command Center and morph it into an Orbital Command.

[Step 3] Build a Starport, a Bunker, a Barracks, a Refinery, and a Factory Tech Lab, while training 3 Marines and 5 SCVs, and morphing the Command Center into an Orbital Command.

[Step 4] Build a Fusion Core and a Starport Tech Lab to unlock high-tech air units, add a Refinery for additional gas income, produce 4 SCVs to support the economy, train 2 Marines for base defense, and construct 1 Siege Tank for strong defensive positioning.

[Step 5] Produce 6 SCVs and 3 Marines while adding a Supply Depot to support the growing army and economy.

[Step 6] Build 3 Supply Depots to increase supply capacity, then simultaneously train a Battlecruiser, 3 Marines, and 3 SCVs.

[Step 7] Produce 4 SCVs to support your economy, construct 4 Supply Depots to maintain headroom, and train 2 Siege Tanks and 3 Marines for a solid defensive posture.

[Step 8] Construct a Barracks with a TechLab, then build two Supply Depots, a Reactor, a Starport with a TechLab, and train one Battlecruiser, four Marines, and one Siege Tank.

[Step 9] Construct your first and second Barracks and first and second Supply Depots, then research Shield Wall and train 7 Marines and 2 Battlecruisers to field a strong ground-and-air army.

[Step 10] Build a Command Center to secure your third expansion, while training a Siege Tank and 7 Marines for ground defense, and construct a Battlecruiser for powerful air support and harassment.

[Step 11] Construct the first and second Supply Depot while training 8 Marines to reinforce your army and secure map control.

[Step 12] Build a Supply Depot to maintain supply, then train 1 Battlecruiser, 7 Marines, and 1 Siege Tank.

[Step 13] Train 7 Marines to reinforce the ground army, produce a Siege Tank for defensive positioning, start building a Battlecruiser for late-game firepower, and morph the Command Center into an Orbital Command for enhanced scanning and supply management.

[Step 14] Build a Supply Depot to maintain production capacity, then immediately train 8 Marines and 1 Battlecruiser to bolster your army with a powerful capital ship.

[Step 15] Build 3 Supply Depots to maintain your production capacity while training 6 Marines for early game pressure or defense.
