# Summary

This build order executes a fast, aggressive bio timing push centered on early Marauders backed by Marines, using a quick double Barracks opening with a Reaper scout and fast Orbital Commands to fuel constant production. The early game features a standard expand (first CC at step 2) with gas-heavy tech: Tech Lab on the first Barracks for Punisher Grenades and Marauder production, a Reactor on the second Barracks for Marine output, and ShieldWall researched to boost Marauder survivability. The tech path remains purely Barracks-based with no Factory or Starport, focusing on Marine-Marauder composition throughout. After building a solid early army and taking a natural expansion at step 9, the build transitions into mass Marine production by attaching Reactors to additional Barracks and churning out waves of Marines, aiming to overwhelm the opponent with sustained bio pressure and map control.

# Details

[Step 1] Produce 5 SCVs while building a Supply Depot, the first and second Barracks, and the first and second Refinery to kickstart your economy and dual-barracks production.

[Step 2] Build a Command Center and a Supply Depot, attach a Tech Lab and a Reactor to your Barracks, then morph the Command Center into an Orbital Command while training 2 SCVs, a Reaper, and 2 Marines, and research Punisher Grenades.

[Step 3] Produce 4 SCVs and construct 2 Supply Depots along with a Barracks, then train 1 Marine and 2 Marauders to establish a solid early-game army.

[Step 4] Build a Tech Lab on the Barracks and morph the Command Center into an Orbital Command. Train 2 SCVs, then produce 3 Marauders and 2 Marines, and research ShieldWall to improve your marauders' survivability.

[Step 5] Construct a Supply Depot to support your growing infrastructure, then produce 4 SCVs to maintain economy while training 3 Marines and 2 Marauders for a solid combat force ready to apply pressure or defend your expansion.

[Step 6] Build 2 Supply Depots, produce 4 SCVs, train 2 Marines, and then train 2 Marauders to strengthen your early army composition.

[Step 7] Produce 3 SCVs and build two Supply Depots, then train 3 Marines and 2 Marauders to reinforce your army.

[Step 8] Produce 5 SCVs to keep the economy growing, build 2 Supply Depots to maintain headroom, and train 2 Marines alongside 2 Marauders to strengthen the bio army.

[Step 9] Take an expansion by building a Command Center, add a Supply Depot, and construct the first and second Barracks to support a push with 4 Marauders and 2 Marines while producing 2 SCVs to maintain economy.

[Step 10] Build 3 Barracks and a Supply Depot, attach Reactors to 2 of the Barracks, then train 4 Marines and 2 Marauders to establish a solid bio-based fighting force.

[Step 11] Morph the Command Center into an Orbital Command while building two Refineries for gas income, then train 6 Marines and 1 Marauder to bolster your army.

[Step 12] Build 2 Supply Depots to sustain your growing army, construct a Barracks with a Reactor attachment for double Marine production, and then train 7 Marines to reinforce your forces.

[Step 13] Construct a Supply Depot to sustain unit production, and train 9 Marines to bolster your army for early pressure or defense.

[Step 14] Construct 2 additional Supply Depots and train 8 Marines to build up your army's size and firepower.

[Step 15] Build 3 additional Supply Depots to maintain a healthy supply buffer, and train 7 Marines to bolster your army for early-game defense or pressure.

[Step 16] Build two Supply Depots and train eight Marines to secure your supply and establish a solid defensive force.

[Step 17] Construct 3 Supply Depots to maintain growing production capacity, while training 6 Marines and 1 Marauder to reinforce the bio army for the next engagement.

[Step 18] Build the first and second Supply Depot to boost supply capacity, and train 8 Marines to reinforce your army for map control or early pressure.

[Step 19] Produce 8 Marines while adding a pair of Supply Depots to support your growing army.

[Step 20] Build 2 Supply Depots to support your growing economy, and train 8 Marines for early defensive pressure or map control.

[Step 21] Attach Reactors to two Barracks and produce 15 Marines to rapidly reinforce your bio army.
