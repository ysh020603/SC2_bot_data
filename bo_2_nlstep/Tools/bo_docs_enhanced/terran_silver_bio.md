# Summary

This build revolves around a pure bio core of Marines and Marauders, aiming for sustained pressure and a heavy timing punch with constant reinforcements. You open with a fast expand, double gas, and a Reaper for early map control, then rush out Concussive Shells and Combat Shield off a Tech Lab and Reactor Rax setup. Mid-game you drop a third CC and mass additional Barracks with Reactors, churning out wave after wave of Marines and Marauders to keep the bio ball rolling. Use this gameplan as your strategic baseline — adapt your decisions based on what you scout and how the game unfolds.

# Details

[Step 1] Begin the opening by placing the first depot to keep up supply, while constantly producing SCVs. Once you have enough minerals, drop the first rax and first refinery, then continue worker production. Follow up by adding a second rax and a second refinery to ramp up infrastructure, all while maintaining steady SCV output.

[Step 2] Upgrade your first Command Center to an Orbital Command while training a Reaper and a couple of Marines for early map control and defense. Keep SCV production humming, then take your natural expansion by dropping a second Command Center. Add a Tech Lab and a Reactor to your Barracks to unlock Marauders and boost Marine production, throw down a Supply Depot to stay safe on supply, and begin researching Concussive Shells to improve kiting potential.

[Step 3] Begin construction on the first rax to kick off infantry production, while continuously pumping SCVs out of the Command Center. Train a Marauder, then a Marine, followed by a second Marauder, and throw down a couple of depots to keep supply flowing.

[Step 4] Research Combat Shield to give your bio an early durability advantage, then start morphing the orbital and add a tech lab onto the first rax. Begin producing a mix of marauders and marines alongside constant SCV production to get a solid bio force rolling.

[Step 5] Keep the worker production going by pumping SCVs while using the rax to mix marines and marauders for a balanced bio composition. Drop a depot to stay ahead on supply.

[Step 6] Keep worker production steady while adding a couple of Marauders to your bio force, bolster the army with a few Marines, and throw down two depots to prevent supply block.

[Step 7] Keep pumping SCVs from the Command Center while reinforcing your bio force with a few Marines and a couple of Marauders from the rax; throw down two depots to stay ahead on supply.

[Step 8] Keep up continuous SCV production while adding a couple of supply depots to avoid getting blocked. From the rax, train a mix of marines and marauders to build a solid bio core.

[Step 9] Drop your third Command Center while keeping workers and marauders in production, then follow up by adding two more Barracks and a Supply Depot. Once the new rax are up, start churning out a mix of Marines and Marauders to bolster your bio army.

[Step 10] Now is the time to really ramp up bio production — throw down several additional Barracks to increase your unit output. Start pumping out a mix of Marines and a couple of Marauders to build a solid combat force, and add Reactors to the new rax for double production. Don’t forget an extra Supply Depot to keep the growing army supplied.

[Step 11] Drop two more Refineries to secure gas income for tech, morph the main CC into an Orbital Command for MULEs and scans, and start producing a Marauder alongside a wave of Marines to reinforce the bio ball.

[Step 12] Start by training several marines, then add a reactor to the barracks to double the output, while also placing two supply depots to ensure you don't get supply blocked.

[Step 13] Throw down a depot to keep supply open while pumping out a sizable wave of Marines to reinforce your army.

[Step 14] Keep pumping out Marines for a solid bio army, and throw down two additional depots to ensure you don't get supply blocked while you reinforce.

[Step 15] Pump out a wave of Marines from the barracks while throwing down a couple of extra depots to keep the supply headroom up for the growing army.

[Step 16] Drop two depots to keep supply flowing while churning out a wave of Marines from the Barracks for a strong early army.

[Step 17] Train a Marauder and several Marines from the rax while dropping a few supply depots to free up supply for the growing army.

[Step 18] Pump out a wave of Marines from the Barracks while throwing down a couple of Supply Depots to free up supply and maintain a solid ground presence.

[Step 19] Throw down a couple of depots to stay ahead on supply, while churning out a solid wave of Marines from your barracks to reinforce your army's numbers.

[Step 20] Throw down two depots to avoid supply blocks, and start cranking out a solid wave of Marines—eight in total—to bolster your army for the next engagement.

[Step 21] Crank out 15 Marines to reinforce your bio force, then add a couple of reactors to your barracks to double your production capacity and keep the reinforcements flowing.
