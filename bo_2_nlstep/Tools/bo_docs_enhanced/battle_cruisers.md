# Summary

This build revolves around a Marine-Siege Tank-Battlecruiser core, aiming for a mid-game timing push that leverages BC air dominance backed by a solid bio-mech ground force. You fast expand after your first rax, grab early gas for a Factory and Starport, then rush a Fusion Core to start pumping BCs while keeping defensive pressure with Marines and Tanks. In the mid-game, you ramp up with a reactor rax for mass Marine production, a tech-lab rax for Combat Shield, a Factory cranking Tanks, and a Starport on a steady BC queue to punch through the opponent. Use this gameplan as your strategic baseline — adapt your decisions based on what you scout and how the game unfolds.

[Step 2] Morph the first Command Center into an Orbital Command while simultaneously dropping the natural expansion, keep constant SCV and Marine production going, add a Refinery for gas, then start the Factory for early tech, and throw down a Supply Depot to avoid supply blocks.

[Step 3] Start a Starport for tech flexibility and throw down a Bunker for early defense. Morph the main Command Center into an Orbital for MULEs, then add a second Barracks to boost marine production alongside a third Refinery to fuel gas-heavy tech. Attach a Tech Lab to the Factory while keeping workers and marines rolling out steadily.

[Step 4] Drop the Fusion Core to set up for Battlecruisers, add a tech lab to the Starport, and throw down a third Refinery for extra gas income. Continue worker production while churning out a couple of Marines for safety, and start the first Siege Tank from the Factory for early defensive firepower.

[Step 5] Keep SCV production humming along while reinforcing with a few Marines and adding a Depot to maintain the supply flow.

[Step 1] Start building a Battlecruiser from the Starport while keeping SCV production from the Command Center and reinforcing with a few Marines from the Barracks; to avoid supply blocks, drop three additional depots during this phase.

[Step 8] Start your first Battlecruiser while setting up a second Barracks with a Tech Lab and adding a Reactor to the initial Barracks for mass Marine production. Drop two Supply Depots, then pump out a Siege Tank from the Factory and several Marines for defense, and don't forget to attach a Tech Lab to the new Starport for future upgrades.

Queue up the first Battlecruiser from the Starport while training a wave of Marines from the existing raxes, then start a second BC and continue reinforcing with more Marines. Add a couple of Supply Depots to keep your supply headroom in check.

[Step 12] Start building your first Battlecruiser from the Starport while simultaneously reinforcing the bio-mech army with a Siege Tank and a wave of Marines. Add a Supply Depot to keep up with the growing army supply.

[Step 13] Morph the CC into an Orbital Command while starting production of a Battlecruiser from the Starport, then pump a handful of Marines from the Barracks to reinforce, and finish the step by building a Siege Tank out of the Factory to solidify the ground defense.
