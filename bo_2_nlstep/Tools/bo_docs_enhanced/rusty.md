# Summary

This build centers on a Marine-Tank-Medivac composition with a Viking for anti-air, executing a sustained timing push that leverages the tank's splash damage and medivac mobility to pressure the opponent. You open with a fast expand and double gas, dropping an Orbital on your natural, then go Factory with Tech Lab for Siege Tanks before adding a Starport and extra Barracks to set up your tech path. In the mid-game, you slap reactors on your rax to pump mass marines while the factory keeps churning out tanks and the starport adds medivacs and vikings, with Combat Shield rolled out to beef up your bio ball. Use this gameplan as your strategic baseline — adapt your decisions based on what you scout and how the game unfolds.
