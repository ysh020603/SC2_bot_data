# Summary

This build revolves around a bio-mech timing push with Marines and Siege Tanks as your core army, threatening a powerful two-base punch by massing infantry behind tank siege lines. You open with a fast expand and an early gas to rush out a Factory, plop a Tech Lab on it, and get your first Tanks out while keeping SCV production constant to stabilize your economy. Mid-game you flood eight-plus Barracks, slapping Reactors on a few for nonstop Marine production, and keep the Factory rolling out Tanks while researching Combat Shield to beef up your bio. Use this gameplan as your strategic baseline — adapt your decisions based on what you scout and how the game unfolds.

# Details

[Step 1] Begin by throwing down a Supply Depot while keeping SCV production constant. Then add your first Barracks and a Refinery for early gas income, all while continuously training workers from the Command Center.

[Step 2] Expand to the natural with the second CC, add a depot for supply, and keep worker production steady while training a few Marines. Once the expansion is underway, drop a Refinery and start the Factory to transition into mech tech.

[Step 3] Keep SCV production rolling while adding several depots to avoid supply blocks. Start a Factory with a Tech Lab attached, setting up for your mech or tech play.

[Step 4] Keep pumping SCVs from the command center to strengthen your economy while producing the first Siege Tank from the factory for early game defense or pressure. Then add a tech lab onto the factory to unlock further tech options, and throw down an extra supply depot to prevent being supply blocked.

[Step 5] Drop a Refinery to secure additional gas income, keep SCV production going, and produce a Siege Tank for defensive support while adding several depots to free up supply.

[Step 6] Add a second Barracks with a Tech Lab, research Combat Shield to toughen up your Marines, pump out a couple of Siege Tanks from the Factory, while keeping worker production steady and dropping an extra Refinery to secure the gas income for your tech.

[Step 7] Morph both Command Centers into Orbitals for faster economy and scans, while pumping out several Siege Tanks from the Factory and a wave of Marines from the Barracks. Add a couple of depots to free up supply as the army grows.

[Step 8] Throw down a massive wave of Barracks — eight total — along with a couple of Supply Depots to support the upcoming bio push and ensure you don't get supply blocked.

[Step 9] Drop two more Barracks and immediately add Reactors to them to ramp up Marine production, while the Factory pumps out three Siege Tanks to support this bio-mech timing push.

[Step 10] Throw down the first Factory and start producing Siege Tanks while keeping the Barracks pumping out a steady stream of Marines for map control and defense.

[Step 11] Continue pumping out a batch of Marines from the Barracks, slap a tech lab onto the Factory to unlock tech, and drop several Supply Depots to keep the supply headroom high.

[Step 12] Throw down a wave of depots—about 10 of them—to break through a supply block and keep the production lines running smoothly.

[Step 13] Add a supply depot to avoid getting blocked, then pump out a few Siege Tanks from the Factory while the Barracks continuously churns out a wave of Marines to support your advancing mech force.

[Step 14] Keep pumping Marines from your Barracks while adding a Siege Tank from the Factory to provide the siege power needed for the upcoming push.

[Step 15] Pump out a pair of Siege Tanks from the Factory while reinforcing with a full round of 8 Marines from the Barracks to bolster your ground force.

[Step 16] Keep churning out marines from the barracks, training a solid squad of them, while also rolling out a tank from the factory for added firepower.

[Step 17] Pump out a wave of Marines from your rax to bolster your army count and maintain map presence.

[Step 18] Expand by dropping another Command Center and immediately morph it into an Orbital Command, while pumping out a large wave of marines from the rax to defend the new base and maintain map presence.
