# Summary

This build revolves around a marine-tank core with raven support for detection and interference, aiming for a safe, defensive macro game that transitions into mech with vikings and liberators for air control. You open with a standard gas-first expand, dropping a rax and two refineries early, then factory into starport while taking a fast natural expansion and producing reapers and hellions for harassment and map presence. In the mid-game, you ramp up bio with stim and combat shield from multiple raxes on reactors, while your factory cranks siege tanks and your starport churns out medivacs, vikings, and later liberators, supported by double e-bays for infantry and vehicle upgrades. Use this gameplan as your strategic baseline — adapt your decisions based on what you scout and how the game unfolds.

# Details

[Step 1] Begin with constant SCV production from the Command Center, throw down a Supply Depot, then build the first Refinery and the first Barracks. Once the Barracks is underway, add a second Refinery while continuing to pump workers to ensure a solid economic foundation for the early game.

[Step 2] Morph your Command Center into an Orbital Command for MULEs, start a Factory for tech, and throw down two supply depots to keep the build flowing. Keep SCV production constant while training a pair of Reapers from the Barracks; once the Factory finishes, produce a Hellion to begin early harassment.

[Step 3] Take your expansion by dropping a second Command Center while maintaining steady SCV production; add a depot to keep supply flowing, then start a Starport and attach Tech Labs to both your Factory and Barracks. From the Factory, produce a Hellion and a Cyclone for early pressure, while the Barracks trains a Reaper for map control, and the Starport builds a Raven to provide detection and interference.

[Step 4] Morph the first Command Center into an Orbital Command for mule drops, then add a Refinery to secure gas income. Keep SCV production going from the main while your Factory produces a Siege Tank for defense. Take your natural expansion with a second Command Center and throw down an extra Supply Depot to avoid getting supply blocked. Continue worker production to saturate the new base once it lands.

[Step 5] Continue pumping SCVs from the Command Center and add a Reactor on the Barracks for faster bio production, while the Starport produces a Raven to provide detection and defensive support. Also throw down an extra Supply Depot to keep up with the growing army, and then start a Siege Tank from the Factory to bolster your ground defense.

[Step 6] Add a couple of supply depots to keep up with production and maintain SCV training from the main base. Start producing a Siege Tank for defensive or siege potential, along with two Vikings for air control and engagement flexibility. Follow up with additional depots and workers to sustain the macro.

[Step 7] Build two additional Barracks and upgrade the first Command Center to an Orbital Command while continuously producing SCVs, then produce a Viking and a Siege Tank for map control and defense, followed by a second Viking.

[Step 8] Drop a pair of Engineering Bays while keeping up worker production, throw down three Refineries to ramp up gas income, start researching Stim from a Tech Lab Barracks, and add a Reactor onto a second Barracks for faster marine output.

[Step 9] Keep pumping SCVs from the main base while adding a reactor to the Barracks and throwing down a few depots to avoid supply blocks. Produce a couple of Vikings for map control and anti-air, and start researching +1 infantry weapons to boost your marine firepower.

[Step 10] Keep SCV production humming while throwing down a pair of Barracks and a Supply Depot. From the Factory, begin Siege Tank production, and reinforce with a few Marines to secure the front.

[Step 11] Start by training a couple of Marines from the Barracks for map presence, then pump a Medivac out of the Starport to support the army. Keep SCV production ramping up from the Command Center while adding three supply depots to avoid being supply blocked as you expand your economy.

[Step 12] Keep pumping SCVs while training five Marines and a Medivac for mobility, throw down two depots to prevent supply block, start Infantry Armor research, and begin Siege Tank production from the Factory to add punch to your army.

[Step 13] Ramp up bio production by adding a reactor to the Barracks for faster Marine production, while keeping the Starport busy building Medivacs for drop play. Throw down a couple of depots to avoid supply blocks and keep reinforcments streaming smoothly.

[Step 14] Begin the mech transition by building the Armory for vehicle upgrades, while pumping out 5 Marines from the barracks and a Siege Tank from the factory. Add 3 more depots to free up supply for the growing army.

[Step 15] Produce a Liberator from the Starport for map control and to harass enemy positions while pumping out a wave of Marines from the Barracks. Research Combat Shield to buff their durability and throw down a couple of extra depots to keep supply from blocking production.

[Step 16] Continue producing Liberators from the Starport while reinforcing infantry by adding a Reactor on the Barracks and churning out several Marines. Throw down two depots to cap supply, and also produce a Siege Tank from the Factory to strengthen your tank count and defensive positioning.

[Step 17] Research +1 infantry and vehicle weapons while reinforcing with several Marines, and add a Viking for anti-air and map vision.

[Step 18] Keep churning out Marines from the rax while adding a Viking from the starport for early air patrol. Throw down a depot to prevent supply block, and drop a factory to transition into mech. As soon as the factory finishes, start a Siege Tank to defend your expansion.

[Step 19] Continue churning out Marines from the Barracks while throwing down a couple of Supply Depots to avoid getting blocked; once resources allow, start producing a Viking from the Starport to gain vision or provide air defense.

[Step 20] Pump out a wave of Marines while researching infantry armor to boost their survivability, and start producing a Viking from the Starport for air control or map vision.

[Step 21] Produce a Siege Tank from the Factory while massing a wave of Marines from the Barracks and add a Tech Lab to the Factory for future upgrades, then start a Viking from the Starport for air control and detection.

[Step 22] Start producing a steady stream of Marines from the Barracks while adding a Reactor to double your marine production. From the Starport, build your first Viking for air control and vision, then continue reinforcing with several more Marines to keep your army count healthy.

[Step 23] Pump out a wave of marines from the Barracks while producing a Liberator and a Viking out of the Starport, and get your vehicle weapons upgrade started to boost the mech damage.
