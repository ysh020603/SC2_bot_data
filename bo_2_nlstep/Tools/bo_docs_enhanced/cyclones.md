# Summary

This build revolves around a Cyclone/Hellion mech composition, using early pressure to secure map control before transitioning into a sustained, macro-oriented factory spam that overwhelms opponents with constant unit production.

It opens with a fast expand into double Orbital Commands, a gas-first Factory with a Tech Lab for Cyclone tech, and a quick third Command Center to fuel the expanding mech economy.

Mid-game, you pump multiple Factories—some with Tech Labs for Blue Flame Hellions and Cyclones, others with Reactors—while researching vehicle weapons upgrades to keep your army hitting hard and staying ahead on supply.

Use this gameplan as your strategic baseline — adapt your decisions based on what you scout and how the game unfolds.

# Details

[Step 1] Start by placing the first depot while pumping out several SCVs from the main, then drop a Command Center to take an early expansion followed by a Barracks and a Refinery. Keep worker production steady, add a second depot, and finish by training the first Marine for light defense or a potential scout denial.

[Step 2] Morph both Command Centers into Orbital Commands while training a few Marines from the Barracks. Start building the first Factory, keep SCV production going, and throw down a Refinery for gas income.

[Step 3] Continue worker production while adding a tech lab to the factory, then throw down two refineries to secure gas income. Drop a third Command Center for the expansion, train a Cyclone for early pressure or defense, and pump out a Marine from the Barracks to round out the army.

[Step 4] Add a Factory to start transitioning into mech, keep SCV production steady, and throw down a couple of depots to avoid supply blocks, then immediately start training a Cyclone for early map control or harassment.

[Step 5] Continue worker production by training 6 SCVs from the command centers, add a tech lab to the Factory to unlock Cyclone tech, then research the Cyclone lock-on damage upgrade and start producing 2 Cyclones. Meanwhile, upgrade the main base to an Orbital Command to boost mining income and provide scans, drop a Refinery to secure additional gas, and begin researching blue flame for Hellions to increase their damage against light units.

[Step 6] Start building your first Factory while continuing worker production, then add a second Factory and train a Hellion for map control or harassment before following up with a Cyclone to apply early mech pressure.

[Step 7] Throw down four depots to free up supply, keep SCV production going from the Command Center, and start building a Cyclone from the Factory for early map control and pressure.

[Step 8] Keep pumping SCVs and throw down a couple of depots to maintain supply headroom, start producing a Cyclone from the Factory while adding both a Reactor and a Tech Lab for flexible tech options, then take a new expansion to fund the growing mech army.

[Step 9] Keep worker production humming in the background while building a pair of Cyclones to apply early pressure. Throw down several depots to avoid supply blocks and maintain a good wall, setting up for sustained factory output.

[Step 10] Keep SCV production humming while throwing down several depots to free up supply, then start a Cyclone for early pressure or defense. Add a second Refinery and an Engineering Bay to set up upgrades, and continue worker production to support your expanding tech.

[Step 11] Continue worker production while adding a couple of depots to keep supply flowing, and start pumping out a few Cyclones and Hellions from the Factory to build up an early mech force.

[Step 12] Start by training an SCV and a Cyclone, then throw down a supply depot. Produce another SCV and Cyclone, followed by a couple of Hellions and a third Cyclone. Morph your Command Center into a Planetary Fortress for a solid defensive position, and build two refineries to secure gas income. Finish the phase by training a fourth Cyclone and one more Hellion.

[Step 13] Start pumping out multiple Factories while producing a couple of Cyclones and a few Hellions to build up a solid mech army.

[Step 14] Drop the first Factory and immediately start a Cyclone, then add two more Factories. Produce additional Cyclones along with a pair of Hellions, and throw down a couple of Supply Depots to keep the supply headroom.

[Step 15] Throw down a few supply depots to maintain headroom while pumping out a handful of hellions from your factories. Add tech labs to both factories to set up the blue flame upgrade, then continue reinforcing with more hellions.

[Step 16] Produce a few Hellions and 4 Cyclones from the Factories while adding two Supply Depots to keep the supply flowing; also attach Tech Labs to both Factories to unlock higher-tier mech upgrades.

[Step 17] Start by putting down multiple armories to set up vehicle upgrades, then build a Hellion and a Cyclone for early pressure, while adding depots as needed to keep supply open.

[Step 18] Produce two Cyclones from the Factory while throwing down a series of supply depots to avoid supply blocks and support the growing mech army, preparing for a mid-game push.

[Step 19] Pump out a couple of Cyclones, each followed by a handful of Hellions, and start researching the vehicle weapons upgrade to boost your mech forces.
