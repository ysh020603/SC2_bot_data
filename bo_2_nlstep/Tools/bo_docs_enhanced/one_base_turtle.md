# Summary

You're locking down on one base with a Marine-Tank core, turtling behind bunkers and siege positions to defend then roll out with a timing push. You go fast Orbital, drop a Refinery early, then Barracks into Factory with a Tech Lab for Tanks, adding a second Barracks with Tech Lab while skipping a natural expansion entirely. Mid-game you keep churning Marines from both Rax and Siege Tanks from the Factory, throwing down Depots to avoid being supply blocked as you mass up for the push. Use this gameplan as your strategic baseline — adapt your decisions based on what you scout and how the game unfolds.

# Details

[Step 1] Start the game by building a Supply Depot while keeping up constant SCV production from the Command Center. Once you have enough minerals, drop a Barracks and a Refinery, then throw down a second Depot to avoid supply blocks. Continue training workers without pause, and after the Barracks finishes, begin training your first Marine for early map control or defense.

[Step 2] Morph the Command Center into an Orbital Command, then kick off Marine production while dropping the first Factory. Keep SCV production going, add a Supply Depot, throw down two Bunkers and a Refinery to secure your position and prepare for tech, then reinforce with a few more Marines.

[Step 3] Drop your first Factory and attach a Tech Lab for Siege Tank production, while continuing steady SCV and Marine training. Start a Siege Tank as the tech lab finishes, then add a second Barracks and a Supply Depot to manage supply. Quickly put another Tech Lab on the new Barracks to tech up your bio force, transitioning into a safe midgame composition with tank support.

[Step 4] Add another rax to ramp up production, then train a couple of Siege Tanks from the factory while reinforcing with a few Marines. Keep up the supply by throwing down two depots as you build up your army for an early push.

[Step 5] Reinforce with several Marines and a couple of Siege Tanks from your production, and throw down an extra depot to keep supply from blocking the push.

[Step 6] Keep pumping out marines to reinforce the bio army, add a couple of supply depots to avoid supply blocks, and start rolling out the first Siege Tank for early positional defense.

[Step 7] Ramp up the bioball by producing several Marines from the rax while the fact pumps out 3 Siege Tanks to form a classic Marine-Tank composition, setting up for a strong mid-game push.

[Step 8] Continue pumping out a steady stream of Marines while adding your first two Siege Tanks for defensive power, and throw down another depot to keep supply flowing.

[Step 9] Crank out a wave of Marines from the rax while the factory produces a couple of Siege Tanks to shore up the defensive line, then reinforce with another round of Marines and a second Tank to round out the mid-game composition.

[Step 10] Add depots as needed while pumping out a wave of Marines from the Barracks and produce your first Siege Tank from the Factory, then follow up with a second Siege Tank supported by additional Marines for a strong early push.
