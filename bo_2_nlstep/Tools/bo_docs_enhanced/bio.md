# Summary

This build revolves around a bio ball of marines and marauders backed by medivacs, with a splash of siege tanks and vikings for a balanced, mid-game timing push. You open with a reaper for scouting into a fast expand, then slap a reactor on your first rax for marine production, followed by a factory for tanks and double starports for medivacs and a viking. In the mid-game, you ramp up to multiple rax with tech labs and reactors, massing marines and marauders while pumping medivacs, and grab stim, combat shield, and concussive shells to boost your push. Use this gameplan as your strategic baseline — adapt your decisions based on what you scout and how the game unfolds.

# Details

[Step 1] Start by throwing down a Depot and begin constant SCV production. Build the first Barracks and a Refinery to secure gas, then add a second Barracks. Once the Barracks are up, train a Reaper for early map control and scouting.

[Step 2] Morph the Command Center into an Orbital Command, pump a couple Marines, and keep up SCV production from the main base. Drop a new Command Center for your expansion, add a Supply Depot to free up supply, then slap a Reactor onto the Barracks for double marine output. Finally, start your first Factory to begin the tech transition into mech.

[Step 3] Keep up worker and marine production while adding a Tech Lab to the Factory for tech access, and throw down a batch of depots to avoid getting supply blocked.

[Step 4] Morph the first Command Center into an Orbital Command for extra scans and mule drops, then begin Siege Tank production from the Factory while pumping out a few Marines from the Barracks for early pressure. Add a Refinery to secure gas, drop a Tech Lab on the Barracks to enable Marauders, and keep steady worker production from the main base.

[Step 5] Add two Starports for air production, throw down another Barracks, and begin researching Concussive Shells to slow down enemy units. Keep SCV production running at the Command Center and train a couple of Marines to bolster your ground army.

[Step 6] Maintain worker production with several SCVs while adding two depots to keep supply flowing. From the Factory, produce a Siege Tank to anchor your army, and from the Barracks, train a couple of Marines and a Marauder. Finally, attach a Tech Lab to the Barracks to unlock further upgrades or more Marauder production.

[Step 7] Kick off Stimpack research from the Barracks tech lab while adding a couple of depots to keep supply healthy, and continue worker production with several SCVs. Reinforce the army by training a few Marines.

[Step 8] Start Combat Shield research to boost your bio's durability, while continuing to pump out Marines from the rax to reinforce your army. Add a couple of depots to keep supply flowing smoothly, and maintain worker production by making two SCVs from the Command Center. Finally, get a Medivac from the Starport for mobility and healing, setting up for future drop plays or to support your ground forces.

[Step 9] Keep SCV production going while training a pair of marines for early defense. To avoid getting supply blocked, throw down a few depots. Then, take an expansion by dropping a new Command Center, and follow up by adding another Barracks to ramp up marine production.

[Step 10] Add a couple of fresh Barracks to your production line, and slap a Reactor on one of them to crank out units faster. From your rax, start pumping out multiple Marauders and Marines, while your Starport produces a Medivac for healing support and a Viking to keep the skies clear.

[Step 11] Add another Barracks and start producing a Marauder, throw down a Supply Depot, then attach a Reactor to that Barracks to pump out several Marines. Follow up by adding a Reactor to the Starport and secure gas income with a couple of additional Refineries.
