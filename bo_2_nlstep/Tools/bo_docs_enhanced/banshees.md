# Summary

This build revolves around a marine-tank-banshee core, leaning on early banshee harass to soften the opponent before rolling into a powerful timing push with sieged tanks and marines. You open with a standard reaper-less expand, dropping your natural CC early, grabbing double gas, and teching through rax into factory and starport—slapping tech labs on both to unlock tanks and banshees. Mid-game you add a couple more rax, keep pumping marines, tanks, and banshees, grab Combat Shield, and take a third while staying aggressive with constant harass and pressure. Use this gameplan as your strategic baseline — adapt your decisions based on what you scout and how the game unfolds.

# Details

[Step 1] Start with a supply depot, then keep SCV production running while throwing down the first barracks and the first refinery to get gas income going. Continue training SCVs steadily, and add a second depot to avoid being supply blocked, establishing a solid foundation for the early build.

[Step 2] Morph your first Command Center into an Orbital Command for faster economy, drop a second CC to take your natural expansion, and throw down a Refinery to start collecting gas. Keep producing workers and marines non-stop while starting your Factory—this sets you up for tech into Siege Tanks or Widow Mines. Add an extra Supply Depot to avoid getting supply blocked during this macro push.

[Step 3] Get the Starport down and throw up a Bunker for defense while you morph the natural Command Center into an Orbital Command. Keep worker production going, train a Marine for safety, and add a second Barracks. Then attach Tech Labs to both the Factory and Starport to enable advanced tech units.

[Step 4] Keep pumping workers from the Command Center while producing a tank from the Factory and a Banshee from the Starport for early harass or map control. Train a couple of Marines from the Barracks to reinforce, and throw down a depot to prevent a supply block.

[Step 5] Keep pumping SCVs while reinforcing with several Marines, throw down a couple of depots to avoid supply block, and produce a Siege Tank to bolster your defensive position.

[Step 6] Add a Barracks and start a Banshee from the existing Starport for early harassment. Keep SCV production going with a couple of workers, then attach a Tech Lab to one Barracks and a Reactor to another. Follow up by building a new Starport and a Refinery for extra gas, then round out the phase with two more workers.

[Step 7] Keep worker production steady and add several depots to avoid supply blocks, while producing a Siege Tank from the Factory and a Banshee from the Starport. Add another Refinery to secure gas income, train a couple of Marines from the Barracks, and attach a Tech Lab to the Starport to unlock the Banshee.

[Step 8] Start Combat Shield research while reinforcing with a wave of Marines from the Barracks. Begin Banshee production at the Starport and throw down a couple of Supply Depots to keep up with the growing army.

[Step 9] Start by training a pair of marines from the barracks, then produce a siege tank from the factory. Add two supply depots to prevent supply block, then start a banshee from the starport for harassment. Finish by training four more marines to reinforce your army.

[Step 10] Keep pumping out Marines from your rax while throwing down several depots to free up supply for the growing army. Meanwhile, produce a Banshee from the starport for harass and a Siege Tank from the factory to anchor your push or defend against counterattacks.

[Step 11] Train a wave of Marines from your Barracks while the Starport begins producing a Banshee for harassment. Follow up with more Marines and throw down a couple of Supply Depots to avoid supply blocks.

[Step 12] Start by queuing up a Siege Tank from the Factory and the first Banshee from the Starport. Throw down a pair of depots to boost supply capacity, then train a handful of Marines from the Barracks. Follow up with a second Banshee and one more depot to keep the production lines flowing.

[Step 13] Keep depots coming to avoid supply blocks while churning out a wave of Marines, then add a Siege Tank from the Factory and a Banshee from the Starport to set up a powerful mid-game timing attack.

[Step 14] Add another rax and churn out Marines while dropping several depots to keep supply flowing; simultaneously, produce a pair of Banshees from the starport and a Siege Tank from the factory to set up a potent harassment force.

[Step 15] Add two more barracks and take the expansion, while producing a handful of Marines alongside a couple of Siege Tanks and several Banshees. Throw down supply depots as needed to support the growing army, and prepare for a timing push that combines tank sieges with banshee harassment.
