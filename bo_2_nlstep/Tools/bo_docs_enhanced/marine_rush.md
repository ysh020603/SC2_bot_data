# Summary

This build is a straight bio all-in, pumping pure marines off multiple rax to hit a fast timing with overwhelming early game pressure. You open with a standard depot and single rax into an Orbital, then immediately drop two more rax skipping gas and expansion for a pure mineral marine flood. Keep all rax hotkeyed and nonstop train marines while spamming supply depots to stay unblocked, as your whole plan hinges on massing a critical count before your opponent can tech up or position AoE. Use this gameplan as your strategic baseline — adapt your decisions based on what you scout and how the game unfolds.

# Details

[Step 2] Morph your first Command Center into an Orbital Command, add two more Barracks, and keep SCV production going while training several Marines to bolster your army.

[Step 3] Keep SCV production going and add a supply depot to avoid getting blocked, then pump out several Marines from your first Barracks while starting your second Barracks to ramp up bio production.

[Step 4] Keep the rax pumping out Marines nonstop while throwing down depots to avoid supply blocks, building a solid core of infantry for early map presence.

[Step 6] Pump out a wave of Marines from your Barracks to start building a solid army presence and prepare for an early push or to shore up your defenses.

[Step 7] Rally a wave of 9 Marines from the rax and throw down a Supply Depot to keep your supply flowing.

[Step 8] Throw down three depots to ensure you don't get supply blocked, then start mass-producing a wave of Marines from the Barracks to build up your army.
