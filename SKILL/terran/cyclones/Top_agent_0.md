# Summary

Fast-expanding Cyclone mech: natural at 16, rush Lock-On damage, scale to 8 Factories with vehicle upgrades. Attack when Lock-On is nearly complete. End-game: mass Cyclones and Hellions.

# Details

Your overall strategy is a fast-expanding Terran mech build that rushes Cyclone production and Lock-On damage, then scales into mass Cyclone/Hellion from many Factories with vehicle upgrades.

* Fast Command Center Before Tech: Open with a Supply Depot, take a very fast natural Command Center at 16 supply, then add the Barracks and early gas. The economy is front-loaded before serious production begins, aiming to support a gas-heavy mech transition.
* Minimal Marine Safety Net: Produce only a small Marine count from the Barracks, mainly to stabilize the early game and unlock the second gas. Marines are not the long-term army plan; they are just an early buffer before Factory units take over.
* Early Cyclone Tech Rush: As soon as the Barracks is ready, start Factory production, add Factory Tech Labs, and prioritize Cyclones. The key early upgrade is Cyclone Lock-On damage, which is treated as the main timing trigger for aggression.
* Gas-Heavy Mech Expansion: Quickly add more Refineries, expand to a third base, and then continue toward a fourth. The plan heavily emphasizes gas income so you can sustain Cyclone production, Hellion support, and vehicle upgrades.
* Cyclone Core with Hellion Support: Build an initial Cyclone group, add one Hellion, then commit heavily into mass Cyclones with priority production up to a very large target. Once a Factory Reactor is available and minerals allow, add many Hellions as a mineral-heavy support unit.
* Factory Explosion and Vehicle Upgrades: Scale production from 2 Factories toward 4, 6, and eventually 8 Factories, mostly supported by Tech Labs. Add an Engineering Bay for Planetary Fortress conversion and later Armories for full vehicle weapon and armor upgrades.
* Lock-On Timing Attack into Mech Snowball: Begin major attacks when the Cyclone Lock-On damage upgrade is nearly complete. From there, keep gathering, reinforcing, repairing, scanning, and finishing the enemy with a mobile upgraded mech army centered on mass Cyclones.
