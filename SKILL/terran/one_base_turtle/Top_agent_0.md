# Summary

This build order is a one-base defensive strategy focused on early turtling with Bunkers and Siege Tanks, delaying expansion indefinitely while teching directly from Barracks to Factory and eventually adding extra Barracks and Factories for mass production. It opens with a fast Refinery and early Marine for map presence, quickly adds an Orbital Command at the natural but never expands beyond that, prioritizing a dense defensive line of Bunkers and Siege Tanks supported by a growing Marine count. The tech path is Barracks → Factory with Tech Labs, then additional Barracks and Factories are constructed to sustain a pure Marine and Siege Tank composition, aiming to lock down the map with siege positions and amass a powerful mid-game army for eventual push-out or denial.

# Details

[Step 1] Begin by training 6 SCVs from the Command Center while sending your first SCVs to construct 2 Supply Depots, a Barracks, and a Refinery, then produce your first Marine for early map presence or defense.

[Step 2] Build a Factory, a Supply Depot, 2 Bunkers, and a Refinery while training 1 SCV and 3 Marines, then morph the Command Center into an Orbital Command to secure your natural expansion and prepare for early pressure.

[Step 3] Produce 2 SCVs and 3 Marines while constructing a Barracks and a Supply Depot. Build a Factory and 2 Factory Tech Labs, then train 1 Siege Tank for early siege support.

[Step 4] Build a Barracks and 2 Supply Depots, then train 5 Marines and 2 Siege Tanks to establish a strong early army.

[Step 5] Build a Supply Depot to maintain production capacity, then train 7 Marines for infantry support and 2 Siege Tanks to establish a strong defensive or siege position.

[Step 6] Construct 3 Supply Depots to support your growing army, while training 5 Marines and 2 Siege Tanks to set up a secure defensive line.

[Step 7] Construct 4 Supply Depots to maintain headroom for production, while training 5 Marines for early pressure or defense and rolling out a Siege Tank to anchor your army with siege capability.

[Step 8] Build a Supply Depot to increase your supply cap, while producing 2 Siege Tanks for siege defense and 7 Marines for a strong bio front line—preparing a solid mid-game army composition for map control.

[Step 9] Build a Supply Depot to support the growing army, then train 11 Marines and 3 Siege Tanks to assemble a powerful siege and infantry force.
