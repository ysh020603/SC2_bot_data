# Summary

One-base Terran turtle: 22 SCVs, Bunker defense, mass Marines and Siege Tanks. Attack only after 18 Marines protect the tank core. Army value threshold 4.

# Details

Your overall strategy is a one-base Terran turtle defense that stays economically compact, fortifies early with a Bunker, masses Marines and Siege Tanks, then attacks only after enough Marines exist to protect the tank core.

* Worker-Limited One-Base Opening: Open with steady SCV production only up to 22 workers, while staying on one Command Center. Build early Supply Depots, a Barracks, and the first Refinery without planning any expansion.
* Fast Orbital and Factory Tech: Once the Barracks is ready, morph into Orbital Command and immediately start Factory tech. The strategy quickly adds the first Factory, then a second Factory, supported by a second Refinery for tank production.
* Early Bunker Turtle: After the Barracks completes, build a Bunker and actively use defensive behavior such as manning bunkers, repairing damaged units or structures, lowering depots, and holding defensive zones.
* Double-Factory Tank Core: Add Tech Labs to the Factories and begin producing Siege Tanks as the main power unit, with a long-term target of up to 20 Tanks.
* Three-Barracks Marine Screen: Scale up to 3 Barracks and continuously produce Marines, aiming for a very large Marine count. Marines serve as the buffer and protection layer for the Siege Tanks.
* Delayed Breakout Attack: Remain defensive until at least 18 Marines have been produced, then launch an attack with the gathered Marine–Tank army. The intended win condition is to survive early pressure, build a compact one-base army, and push out once the tank force has enough infantry cover.
