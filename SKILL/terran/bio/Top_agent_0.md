# Summary

Flexible bio macro: Reaper-expand, early Tanks, upgraded Marine/Marauder/Medivac with situational Vikings and anti-cloak responses. Third base at ~40 workers. Attack at army value 26.

# Details

Your overall strategy is a flexible Terran bio macro build that opens Reaper-expand, stabilizes with early Marines and Siege Tanks, then scales into upgraded Marine/Marauder/Medivac with situational Viking and anti-cloak responses.

* Reaper Expand Opening: Start with a 13 Supply Depot, first Barracks, early Refinery, and one Reaper. Use the Reaper as the first active unit while preparing a normal fast second Command Center unless a rush is detected.
* Rush Bunker Adjustment: If early aggression is detected, abandon the greedy expansion plan, cancel the exposed Command Center, build a defensive Bunker at the ramp, add a second Barracks, and prioritize a couple of Marines to survive the rush.
* Factory Stabilization: After the Barracks setup, add a Reactor, build a Factory with a Tech Lab, and produce early Siege Tanks. The first Tanks are mainly for defensive stability before the bio army fully comes online.
* Bio Tech Core: Add Barracks Tech Labs and research Concussive Shells, Stimpack, and Combat Shield. This turns the army into a Marauder-heavy bio force supported by Marines rather than a pure Marine flood.
* Starport Support Layer: Build a Starport, start Medivac production, and add a small Viking count. If the enemy shows important air or high-tech threats such as Colossi, Void Rays, Carriers, Tempests, Brood Lords, Ravens, Medivacs, or similar targets, increase Viking production heavily.
* Three-Base Barracks Scaling: Once worker count reaches about 40, expand to a third base, grow toward 5 Barracks, then spend excess minerals on up to 8 Barracks and more Reactors. The final composition aims toward a large Marine/Marauder force, up to 6 Medivacs, 2 Siege Tanks, and situational Vikings.
* Anti-Cloak Emergency Response: If Dark Shrine, Dark Templar, or Banshee tech is detected, add an Engineering Bay, Missile Turrets at the entrance and mineral lines, plus Raven production from Starport tech, giving you detection and defensive coverage against invisible threats.
