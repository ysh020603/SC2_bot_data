# Summary

Two-base Marine + Siege Tank macro build. Tech up via 5 Barracks (1 Tech Lab + Reactors) and 3 Factory Tech Labs, prioritising Marine buffer first, then mass-Tank production. End-game goal: 20 Siege Tanks and 100 Marines.

# Details

Your overall strategy is a "Zero-Marine Tank-First" build, optimized to completely shut down early Terran aggression by rushing Siege Tanks. You will treat the initial Barracks purely as a tech requirement and only transition into a Marine bio-force once the defensive Tank line is fully established.

* Universal Economy & Supply Control:
  - Strict SCV Saturation: Monitor the `current/ideal` worker count. Halt SCV production at exactly 44 SCVs.
  - Proactive Supply Buffer: Maintain a high supply buffer. Once supply reaches 40, always keep at least 2 Supply Depots under construction.
  - Resource Rebalancing: If gas floats >500, immediately drop additional Factories with Tech Labs. If minerals float >600 later in the game, drop extra Barracks.

* Phase 1: Pure Tank Tech Rush (Zero-Marine Opening):
  - Build Order: Supply Depot -> Barracks -> Refinery.
  - CRITICAL TECH RULE: Treat the 1st Barracks STRICTLY as a tech building. Do NOT train any Marines from it during this early phase. Save all minerals.
  - Morph Command Center to Orbital Command once the Barracks completes.
  - Build your 1st Factory the exact second you accumulate 100 gas. Attach a Tech Lab immediately upon completion.
  - Continuously queue `Train Siege Tank (Priority)` to force all early gas and minerals into your core defense.

* Phase 2: Stabilization & Safe Expansion:
  - Rely entirely on your first few Siege Tanks (assisted by SCV repair if necessary) to hold the ramp and repel early rushes.
  - Do NOT expand immediately. Only after you have successfully produced and deployed at least 3 to 4 Siege Tanks, construct your 2nd Command Center (Expansion).

* Phase 3: Marine Transition & Macro Escalation:
  - ONLY after the 2nd Command Center is started and your Tank line is stable, begin your bio transition.
  - Build up to a total of 5 Barracks. Attach Reactors to 4 of them, and 1 Tech Lab for Stimpack research.
  - NOW begin continuously training Marines to serve as a mobile meatshield for your Tanks.
  - Scale up to 3 Factories (all with Tech Labs) to increase Siege Tank output.
  - End Goal: Push with a maxed-out composition of 20 Siege Tanks and 100 Marines, supported by a healthy economy of exactly 44 SCVs.
