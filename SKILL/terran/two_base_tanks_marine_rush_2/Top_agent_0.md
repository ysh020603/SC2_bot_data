# Summary

Two-base Marine + Siege Tank macro build. Tech up via 5 Barracks (1 Tech Lab + Reactors) and 3 Factory Tech Labs, prioritising Marine buffer first, then mass-Tank production. End-game goal: 20 Siege Tanks and 100 Marines.

# Details

Your overall strategy is a Marine and Siege Tank macro build, with a strict emphasis on surviving early pressure through a localized early Marine burst before transitioning into a robust Factory/Tank infrastructure:

* Universal Economy & Supply Control:
  - Strict SCV Saturation: Monitor the `current/ideal` worker count. Strictly halt SCV production once you reach your strategic cap of 44 SCVs. 
  - Dynamic Supply & Smooth Spending: Maintain a healthy supply buffer (8-15 early, up to 25 late). Construct Supply Depots incrementally to avoid resource spikes, and NEVER build Depots once the 200 supply cap is reached.
  - Resource Rebalancing: If gas floats >800, temporarily halt Tank production and dump resources into Marines and additional Command Centers. 

* Opening & Early Defensive Burst (Anti-Rush Adaptation): 
  - Start with a Supply Depot at 14 supply. 
  - Once the depot finishes, construct your first Barracks. 
  - Take your first gas Refinery (Delayed Gas). 
  - Morph your Command Center into an Orbital Command once the Barracks finishes.
  - Immediately construct a 2nd Barracks and attach a Reactor to your 1st Barracks. Prioritize constant Marine production from these structures to build a solid early defensive buffer.

* Delayed Expansion & Tech Transition: 
  - Only after establishing this initial Marine defense buffer (approx. 6-10 Marines) and securing the high ground, construct your second Command Center (Expansion).
  - Take your second gas Refinery.
  - Construct your first Factory, immediately attach a Tech Lab to it, and begin Siege Tank production.

* Production Escalation: 
  - Scale your infantry infrastructure up to a total of 5 Barracks (all with Reactors). 
  - Aggressively scale your mechanized infrastructure up to 3 Factories (all with Tech Labs).
  - If minerals float >600 despite constant production, use Macro Scaling to drop additional Barracks or Factories immediately.

* Step-by-Step Expansion: 
  - Expand to a third and fourth base dynamically when it is safe to do so. You must establish, defend, and saturate each new base one at a time before starting the next Command Center.

* End Goal: 
  - Continually train units non-stop until you reach a cap of 20 Siege Tanks and 100 Marines, supported by a strong economy of up to 44 SCVs.
