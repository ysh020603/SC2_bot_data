# Summary

Two-base Marine + Siege Tank macro build. Tech up via 5 Barracks (1 Tech Lab + Reactors) and 3 Factory Tech Labs, prioritising Marine buffer first, then mass-Tank production. End-game goal: 20 Siege Tanks and 100 Marines.

# Details

Your overall strategy is a Marine and Siege Tank macro build, heavily optimized for safety against early rushes. It relies on a fast single Factory to get an early Siege Tank out, which acts as your ultimate defensive anchor before transitioning into a massive two-base macro push.

* Universal Economy & Supply Control:
  - Strict SCV Saturation: Monitor the `current/ideal` worker count. Strictly halt SCV production at 44 SCVs.
  - Proactive Supply Buffer: Maintain a high supply buffer. Once supply reaches 40, always keep at least 2 Supply Depots under construction simultaneously to prevent supply blocks during early attacks.
  - Resource Rebalancing: If gas floats >500, immediately drop additional Factories/Tech Labs. If minerals float >500, drop extra Barracks.

* Opening & Safe Tech Rush (Anti-Rush Core):
  - Start with a Supply Depot at 14 supply, then 1st Barracks, then 1st gas Refinery.
  - Morph your Command Center to an Orbital Command once the Barracks finishes.
  - Immediately build a 2nd Barracks to maintain continuous Marine production (Do not build early Reactors).
  - Tech Priority: Do NOT expand yet. As soon as you hit 100 gas, build your 1st Factory. Instantly attach a Tech Lab when it finishes.
  - CRITICAL - The Anchor Tank: As soon as the Factory Tech Lab finishes, you MUST issue the task `Train Siege Tank (Priority)` to force gas allocation. Getting 1-2 Siege Tanks out immediately is the key to holding any early rush.

* Dynamic Expansion & Escalation (Reactive Phase):
  - Monitor Enemy Intelligence. If the enemy is rushing (high early unit count), absolutely halt any plans for a 2nd Command Center. 
  - Only after your Anchor Tank is out and you have a physical buffer of at least 6-10 Marines defending your ramp, build your 2nd Command Center (Expansion).
  - Take your 2nd gas Refinery only after the expansion is started to preserve early minerals.

* Mid/Late Game Production:
  - Scale infantry infrastructure to 5 Barracks (1 Tech Lab for Stimpack, 4 Reactors).
  - Scale mechanized infrastructure to 3 Factories (all Tech Labs).
  - Continually train units non-stop until you reach your cap of 20 Siege Tanks and 100 Marines, supported by exactly 44 SCVs.
