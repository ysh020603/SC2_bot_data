# Summary

Two-base Marine + Siege Tank macro build. Tech up via 5 Barracks (1 Tech Lab + Reactors) and 3 Factory Tech Labs, prioritising Marine buffer first, then mass-Tank production. End-game goal: 20 Siege Tanks and 100 Marines.

# Details

Your overall strategy is a Marine and Siege Tank macro build, heavily optimized for safety against early rushes and timing attacks by smoothing the mineral-to-gas ratio and prioritizing immediate defensive units and tech before expanding.

* Universal Economy & Supply Control:
  - Strict SCV Saturation: Monitor the `current/ideal` worker count. Strictly halt SCV production at 44 SCVs.
  - Proactive Supply (CRITICAL): Maintain a high supply buffer. Once supply reaches 40, always keep at least 2 Supply Depots under construction simultaneously to prevent severe supply blocks. Never build Depots at 200 supply.
  - Resource Rebalancing: If gas floats >500, temporarily halt gas mining or instantly drop additional Factories/Tech Labs. If minerals float >500, drop extra Barracks.

* Opening & Safe 2-Rax Defense (Anti-Rush):
  - Start with a Supply Depot at 14 supply.
  - Build your 1st Barracks.
  - Build your 1st gas Refinery.
  - Morph your Command Center to an Orbital Command once the Barracks finishes.
  - Immediately build a 2nd Barracks. Do NOT attach a Reactor early on, as it halts production. Train Marines continuously one-by-one from both bare Barracks to guarantee an unbreakable early defensive wall.

* Tech Before Expansion (Anti-Timing):
  - Do NOT queue a 2nd Command Center yet. 
  - As soon as you accumulate 100 gas, build your 1st Factory. 
  - Attach a Tech Lab to the Factory immediately upon completion and start continuous Siege Tank production.
  - Only after the Factory is under construction and you have a physical buffer of at least 6-10 Marines, build your 2nd Command Center (Expansion).

* Production Escalation:
  - Take your 2nd gas Refinery ONLY after the 2nd Command Center is started.
  - Scale your infantry infrastructure up to 5 Barracks (attach Reactors to 4 of them, 1 Tech Lab for Stimpack).
  - Scale your mechanized infrastructure up to 3 Factories (all with Tech Labs).

* End Goal:
  - Continually train units non-stop until you reach a cap of 20 Siege Tanks and 100 Marines, supported by a strong economy of exactly 44 SCVs.
