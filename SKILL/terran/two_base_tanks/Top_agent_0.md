# Summary

Two-base Marine + Siege Tank macro build. Tech up via 5 Barracks (1 Tech Lab + Reactors) and 3 Factory Tech Labs, prioritising Marine buffer first, then mass-Tank production. End-game goal: 20 Siege Tanks and 100 Marines.

# Details

Your overall strategy is a Marine and Siege Tank macro build, with a strict emphasis on the developmental sequence between Barracks/Factories and Marines/Tanks:

* Universal Economy & Supply Control (CRITICAL):
  - Strict SCV Saturation: Monitor the `current/ideal` worker count to saturate bases optimally, but strictly halt SCV production once you reach your strategic cap of 44 SCVs. 
  - Dynamic Supply & Smooth Spending: Maintain a healthy supply buffer (8-15 early, up to 25 late). Construct Supply Depots incrementally to avoid resource spikes, and NEVER build Depots once the 200 supply cap is reached.
  - Resource Rebalancing: If gas floats >800, temporarily halt Tank production and dump resources into Marines and Command Centers. 

* Opening & Tech: At the very beginning of the game, issue a single, isolated task to build a Supply Depot. Then build a Barracks, followed by your first gas Refinery (Delayed Gas). Immediately expand to a second base. Once the Barracks finishes, morph Command Centers to Orbital Commands and start your first Factory.
* Early Marine Buffer & Setup: Train a couple of initial Marines for defense. Grab your second gas Refinery. Begin scaling your infantry infrastructure up to 5 Barracks (using Smooth Spending—do not queue all 5 at once if it starves Marine production), using a mix of 1 Tech Lab to research Combat Shield and Reactors for the rest. Rapidly pump out a solid number of Marines early on to serve as a defensive buffer.
* Simultaneous Factory & Tank Push: While building the initial Marine buffer, aggressively push Factory development. As soon as the first Factory finishes, attach a Tech Lab and start Siege Tanks. Prioritize scaling up to 3 Factories, all with Tech Labs, as early as possible. If minerals float >600, use Macro Scaling to ensure infrastructure is dropped immediately.
* Production Shift: Once you have accumulated enough Marines to secure your defense, shift your primary focus and resources toward mass-producing Siege Tanks, while maintaining steady Marine reinforcement.
* Step-by-Step Expansion: Expand to a third and fourth base dynamically when it is safe to do so. You must establish, defend, and saturate each new base one at a time before starting the next Command Center.
* End Goal: Continually train units non-stop until you reach a cap of 20 Siege Tanks and 100 Marines, supported by a strong economy of up to 44 SCVs.
