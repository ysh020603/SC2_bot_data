# Summary

All-in Marine rush: mineral-only economy capped at 20 SCVs, 6 Barracks producing Marines non-stop. Skip gas, skip second base, launch a 20-Marine zone attack across the map. Designed to end the game in the early phases.

# Details

Your overall strategy is a highly aggressive Marine all-in rush, with a strict emphasis on sacrificing long-term macro development to overwhelm the opponent with an early, massive tier-one infantry swarm. Standard macro rules are heavily modified to fit this all-in parameter:

* Opening & Economy (Gas & SCV Constraints): Start with a Supply Depot at 14 supply. As soon as the depot finishes, construct your first Barracks. Strictly enforce the Delayed Gas principle by taking ZERO gas Refineries; keep this a purely mineral-only economy. For SCV Saturation, override standard rules: halt SCV production exactly at 20 workers. Do not produce any more SCVs, channeling all subsequent resources directly into the army.
* Production Escalation & Smooth Spending: Once the first Barracks is ready, morph your Command Center into an Orbital Command to utilize MULEs. Begin scaling up to a total of 6 Barracks. You must apply Smooth Resource Spending here: do not drop all 5 extra Barracks simultaneously if it prevents you from affording Marines or your next Supply Depot. Build them incrementally as mineral income allows.
* Supply & Macro Scaling: Maintain an aggressive dynamic supply buffer (around 8-15) so Marine production is never blocked. If your supply cap hits 200, absolutely halt Supply Depot construction. If your mineral bank floats above 600 despite constant Marine production (Macro Scaling rule), instantly drop additional Barracks to convert the float into pure combat units.
* Scouting & Utility: Send out an early worker scout as soon as your first Supply Depot begins. Rely on MULE drop-downs to sustain your massive 6-Barracks production cycle, and save energy for Orbital Scans after the 5-minute mark if you need vision or detection.
* Expansion & Tactics (Anti-Expansion Rule): The Step-by-Step Expansion rule is strictly disabled. Do NOT expand to a second base. Gather your steadily growing army safely at your natural expansion. Once your army reaches exactly 20 Marines, launch your all-in zone attack across the map.
* Micro Maneuvers (Dodge Ramp): Pay close attention to enemy ramp defenses. If the opponent (Protoss) casts Forcefields to block their main ramp, your forces must execute a tactical retreat back to your natural gather point to avoid being trapped or out-ranged, resuming the push only once the path is clear.
* End Goal: Continually stream Marines non-stop out of your 6 Barracks (queueing incrementally, not in massive pulse-spending blocks) to completely overrun the enemy base and end the game decisively in the early phases.
