# Summary

Safe TvT Raven opening: double-gas Reaper–Hellion map control, defensive Cyclone, fast 2 Ravens, then Marine–Tank–Viking with upgrades. Attack once Stimpack is ready.

# Details

Your overall strategy is a safe Terran-vs-Terran Raven opening that uses early Reaper-Hellion map control, a defensive Cyclone, fast Ravens, and then transitions into Marine-Tank-Viking production with upgrades.

* Double-Gas Reaper-Hellion Opening: Open with a Supply Depot, early gas, Barracks, second gas, and fast Factory. Keep gas mining tightly managed so you can afford early tech, then produce 3 Reapers and 2 Hellions for scouting, map control, and protection against early TvT pressure.
* Factory Expand Safety: Take the natural Command Center after the Factory setup, while morphing Orbitals once the Barracks is ready. Build a Cyclone as the first major defensive tech unit, giving you a safer answer to early air, Reaper pressure, or aggressive Terran openings.
* Raven Tech-Lab Phase: Add a Starport and use add-on swapping so the Starport can access a Tech Lab. Produce 2 Ravens as a core safety and control tool before committing heavily into later air production.
* Third Command Center & Viking Transition: Around the 4:20 timing, start a third Command Center. After the Ravens are secured, transition the Starport onto a Reactor and begin producing Vikings, aiming for an initial count of 6 to contest air control in TvT.
* Bio-Mech Production Scaling: After the first Vikings, add more Barracks, double Engineering Bays, extra gas, and eventually a second Factory. Scale toward a 5-Barracks, 2-Factory, 1-Starport setup, with Reactors on Barracks, Tech Labs on Factories, and a Reactor on the Starport.
* Upgrade-Backed Marine-Tank-Air Army: Prioritize Stimpack, Infantry Weapons/Armor, Combat Shield, Vehicle Weapons, and later Ship Weapons. The final army plan centers on mass Marines, Siege Tanks, Medivacs, Vikings, and a small Liberator count, with attacking enabled once Stimpack is ready.
