# Summary

Safe TvT Raven opening: double-gas Reaper–Hellion map control, defensive Cyclone, fast 2 Ravens, then Marine–Tank–Viking with upgrades. Attack once Stimpack is ready.

# Details

Your overall strategy is a safe Terran-vs-Terran Raven opening that uses early Reaper-Hellion map control, a defensive Cyclone, fast Ravens, and then transitions into Marine-Tank-Viking production with upgrades. 

* Universal Economy & Supply Control (CRITICAL): 
  - SCV Saturation: Constantly monitor the `current/ideal` worker count for each base. Train SCVs continuously until the current worker count matches the ideal count, then pause to save resources and supply. 
  - Dynamic Supply Buffer & Smooth Spending: Maintain a dynamic supply buffer of 8–15 in the early/mid-game, and 15–25 during late-game production. Build Supply Depots in small, incremental batches (e.g., 1-2 at a time) to avoid pulse spending that disrupts army production.
  - Absolute Supply Cap: Once the maximum supply cap reaches 200, completely halt all Supply Depot construction.

* Double-Gas Reaper-Hellion Opening: Open with a Supply Depot, early gas, Barracks, second gas, and fast Factory. Keep gas mining tightly managed so you can afford early tech, then produce 3 Reapers and 2 Hellions for scouting, map control, and protection against early TvT pressure.
* Factory Expand Safety & Step-by-Step Expansion: Take the natural Command Center after the Factory setup, while morphing Orbitals once the Barracks is ready. Ensure this natural base is established, defended, and fully saturated before planning the next expansion. Build a Cyclone as the first major defensive tech unit.
* Raven Tech-Lab Phase: Add a Starport and use add-on swapping so the Starport can access a Tech Lab. Produce 2 Ravens as a core safety and control tool before committing heavily into later air production.
* Third Command Center & Viking Transition: Around the 4:20 timing, start a third Command Center. Adhere strictly to the step-by-Step expansion rule: secure and saturate this base before even considering a fourth. After the Ravens are secured, transition the Starport onto a Reactor and begin producing Vikings, aiming for an initial count of 6 to contest air control.
* Bio-Mech Production Scaling & Macro Scaling: After the first Vikings, gradually add more Barracks, double Engineering Bays, extra gas, and eventually a second Factory. If you notice your mineral bank floating above 600, immediately execute Macro Scaling by adding additional Barracks or Factories to convert excess resources into production capacity. Scale toward a 5-Barracks, 2-Factory, 1-Starport setup. Only take additional gas Refineries when your tech and Tank/Air production demand it, following the delayed gas principle to protect mineral income.
* Upgrade-Backed Marine-Tank-Air Army: Prioritize Stimpack, Infantry Weapons/Armor, Combat Shield, Vehicle Weapons, and later Ship Weapons. The final army plan centers on mass Marines, Siege Tanks, Medivacs, Vikings, and a small Liberator count, with attacking enabled once Stimpack is ready.
