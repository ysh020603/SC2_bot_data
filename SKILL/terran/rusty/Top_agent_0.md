# Summary

Gas-first Marine–Siege Tank timing: expand after first Marine, 2 Hellion bridge, mass Tanks with Medivacs and reactive Vikings/Ravens. Attack at army value 50–80 (random).

# Details

Your overall strategy is a gas-first Terran Marine–Siege Tank timing build that expands after the first Marine, stabilizes with Tanks and limited Hellions, then scales into Marine/Tank/Medivac pressure with reactive Vikings, Ravens, and missile turrets when needed.

* Gas-First Barracks Setup: Open with a Supply Depot at 13, take early gas around 16 supply, then build a Barracks once the Depot exists. Add a second Depot while the Barracks is still finishing, and begin Orbital Command morphing once the Barracks is ready.
* Marine-Gated Natural Expansion: Produce an initial Marine before taking the natural expansion. Keep SCV production going toward a two-base economy, with the worker target eventually reaching about 44 SCVs.
* Early Factory and Hellion Bridge: After the Barracks finishes, move quickly into Factory tech. Produce up to 2 Hellions only as an early bridge before the Factory Tech Lab is ready, then switch the Factory into Siege Tank production.
* Two-Factory Tank Core: Once the first Siege Tank exists, add a second Factory, go up to four Refineries, and attach Tech Labs to support continuous Tank production. The long-term mech target is up to 20 Siege Tanks.
* Bio and Starport Support Layer: Add more Barracks until reaching 5 total, research Combat Shield from a Barracks Tech Lab, and add Reactors for Marine production. Build a Starport for Medivacs, with a target of up to 6 Medivacs for army mobility and sustain.
* Reactive Anti-Air and Detection Defense: If enemy air tech or key air threats appear, add Vikings in stages, scaling from 1 Viking toward 3 or even 10 depending on enemy air presence. If Dark Shrine, Dark Templar, or Banshees are detected, respond with an Engineering Bay, Missile Turrets, extra Starport tech, and up to 2 Ravens.
* Randomized Marine–Tank Attack Timing: Gather and attack once the army reaches a randomly selected strength threshold between 50 and 80. The final army direction is mass Marines supported by Siege Tanks, Medivacs, and situational Vikings, with a third expansion added after the core production structure is already established.
